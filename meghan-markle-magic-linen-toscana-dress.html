Meghan Markle's $89 Linen Dress Is Now in New Colors

If you're looking for some new spring dresses to add to your closet, we have a feeling you won't want to miss out on this new drop from MagicLinen. The European clothing and bedding brand just launched its popular linen Toscana dress that Meghan Markle wore back in 2020 in three new colors — and it might just sell out (again).

When Meghan was spotted in the breezy white linen dress in Los Angeles two years ago, the Internet practically went into a frenzy. The simple-but-chic dress is a warm weather staple anyone can wear and add to their wardrobe — especially at its $89 price point (it was even cheaper in 2020 at just $76). It unsurprisingly sold out within hours after the sighting; since then MagicLinen has aptly renamed the dress the Royal Toscana and released it in several new colors, including the clay red, blush pink, and teal blue, available today.

Made from 100 percent European flax linen, the sleeveless dress has an asymmetrical hem, two pockets, and a loose fit. The brand notes that it's slightly transparent and recommends wearing neutral-colored undergarments with it. It's available in sizes XS to XL and runs a bit large, according to the brand.
