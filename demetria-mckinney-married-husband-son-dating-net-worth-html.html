American Actress Demetria McKinney is best known for her portrayal of Janine Payne in Tyler Perry's TBS series House of Payne (2006-2012).

After the TBS series came to an end, she began appearing in the Rickey Smiley Show. Her other series include Superstition (2017-2018), Saints & Sinners (2016-2019), The Quad (2017-2018), and A House Divided (2019), to name a few.

Is Demetria McKinney Married? Husband

Demetria McKinney is not married yet. However, she came very close once. 

She was in a decade-long relationship with a TV producer, Roger Bobb. 

The two met around 2006 at Tyler Perry Studio while Demetria was working in the House of Payne. At that time, Robb was the executive vice present of the Tyler Perry Studio. So, their relationship was kept a secret for a long time.

Her Children

Demetria is a mother to two boys. She gave birth to her first child, a son, at the age of seventeen in 1997 with her ex-boyfriend.

Age
