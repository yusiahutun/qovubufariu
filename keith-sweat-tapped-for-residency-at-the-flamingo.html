Keith Sweat Tapped For Residency At The Flamingo

LAS VEGAS (CelebrityAccess) — Multi-platinum recording soul/R&B artist Keith Sweat will mark the 30th anniversary of his debut album “Make It Last Forever” with a short residency at the storied Flamingo Hotel in Las Vegas.

“Keith Sweat: Last Forever” will start on January 17, 2017 and will last through February 4, 2017 at the resort's 700-capacity Donny and Marie Showroom.

“This is what I like to call the second act of my career. This is the most exciting moment in my life. I cannot wait to hit the Flamingo stage," Keith Sweat said in announcing the gig.

Opening for Sweat will be comedian and long-serving MADtv cast member Aries Spears. – Staff Writers
