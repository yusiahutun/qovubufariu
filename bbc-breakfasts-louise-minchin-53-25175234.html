BBC Breakfast's Louise Minchin, 53, shows off ageless beauty as she wows in glam dress

Louise Minchin, 53, has wowed fans with a stunning red dress on her social media, and shared her excitement about watching Dan Walker dance.

It's a very glam look for the former BBC Breakfast presenter, who seems to be loving life away from the iconic red sofa.

She captioned the pics: "Anyone else got that Friday Feeling?"

"I am so looking forward to seeing Dan Walker and the wonderful Nadiya Bychkova dancing the foxtrot," she added.

Despite leaving the show Louise is very much still in touch with her old colleague: "I had a catch up with them on the phone today and they LOVE it!"

"I will be talking about them and all things Strictly tomorrow with Claudia Winkleman on BBC Radio 2. Have a great weekend and let me know what you think of Strictly!"

The presenter looked beautiful in a low cut red dress, teamed with natural make-up and a delicate chain necklace.

Her blonde locks were styled in gentle waves.

Fans loved her new look and left plenty of comments praising Louise's style.

"Looking amazing!" praised one, and another added: "Looking gorgeous as ever Louise!" with a fire and rose emoji.

"We're waiting for you to do Strictly next year" added one hopefully.

"What an incredibly beautiful lady you are" praised another.

"You're looking fabulous! Obviously not having to wake up at silly o'clock works. As for Strictly, it's the best ever" exclaimed one fan.

"No early alarms really suits you, Mrs Glam" said another viewer.

There have been rumours swirling that Louise could be heading into the I'm A Celebrity castle, but fans are hoping she will do Strictly next year instead.

Louise is a keen fitness fanatic, and often shares posts about running, swimming and cycling.

As yet she hasn't announced any new projects, so I'm A Celeb may still happen.

She has been sending her well wishes to Dan throughout the show and praising her former colleague's efforts on the dance floor, and raved about his Paso Doble last week.

It will be a Sleeping Beauty themed foxtrot for Dan this week for Movie Week.
