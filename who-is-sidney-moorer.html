Who Is Sidney Moorer? Wife Tammy Moorer, Where Are They Now?

In this article, we shall discuss Who Is Sidney Moorer? Where Are They Now, Wife Tammy Moorer, Affair With Heather Elvis, their arrest, charges, & More…

Who Is Sidney Moorer?

Sidney Moorer is a citizen of the United States who was a repairman or a welding contractor by profession. His full name is Sidney St. Clair Moorer. 

He came into the limelight after being arrested for the disappearance and alleged murder of a woman named Heather Elvis back on 18th December 2013, with whom he had an affair.

This entire incident was narrated in an episode of ABC 20/20 titled “The Devil’s Triangle”. The episode was first telecasted in May 2021 but was available for a rewatch on 19th August 2022.

Sidney Moorer’s Wife Tammy Moorer, Children

Sidney and Tammy Caison first met in 1997, and soon after that, they started dating each other even though she was four years older than him. The couple got married in the March of 1998, and have three children, including two sons and a daughter.

Sidney Moorer And Tammy Moorer’s Age

Sidney is currently 46 years old while his wife Tammy is 50 years old.

Sidney Moorer’s Affair With Heather Elvis

Sidney cheated on his wife, and had affairs with other women, including Heather Elvis, a bar hostess, who was only 20 years old at the time of their affair, and another unknown woman.

The matter became serious when his wife Tammy came to know about her husband’s affair with Heather only after three months of their being together, in October 2013.

Sidney Moorer’s Wife Tammy Moorer’s Reaction To Husband’s Affair

Tammy took some extreme steps to prevent Sidney to meet Heather, by confiscating his mobile phone, handcuffing him to the bed at night, and physically abusing him. 

She even threatened Heather and tried possible ways to bring her in a bad light by calling her a stalker. Sources also claim that the 20-year-old was pregnant with Sidney’s child at that time.

Sidney Moorer’s Alleged Girlfriend Heather Elvis’s Disappearance

It was their illicit affair and Heather’s pregnancy, that caused Tammy to take the extreme step to remove Heather from her way by possibly abducting her with the help of her husband Sidney. Heather was last seen on 13th December 2013.

The police investigation into the matter revealed that several calls were exchanged between Sidney and Heather on the night of her kidnapping.

Also, his truck was found near the place where Heather was last seen indicating his involvement in her disappearance. She is believed to have been murdered by the couple, though her body was never found.

Sidney Moorer And Wife Tammy Moorer’s Arrest, Charges

After around four months after Heather’s disappearance in February 2014, Sidney and his wife Tammy were arrested by the local police. They were charged with the murder and kidnapping of Heather. 

They were also found obstructing justice and were charged with two counts of indecent exposure. However, the charges of murder and indecent exposure were dismissed by the prosecutors. Later in April 2018, an additional count of conspiracy to kidnap was indicted on them by the grand jury. 

Finally, in 2018, Tammy was found guilty of kidnapping and conspiracy of kidnapping and was sentenced to 30 years of imprisonment for each. 

Sidney, on the other hand, was charged with obstruction of justice in 2017 and found guilty of kidnapping in 2019. He was sentenced to 30 years of imprisonment for each, in addition to ten years for obstruction of justice.

Where Is Sidney Moorer And Wife Tammy Moorer Now? 

Today, Sidney is serving his punishment at the Lee Correctional Institution in Bishopville, South Carolina, while his wife Tammy is serving at the Leath Correctional Institution in Greenwood. Both of them are still trying to prove their innocence.
