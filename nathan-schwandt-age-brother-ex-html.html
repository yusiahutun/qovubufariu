Nathan Schwandt Dating Status With Jeffree Star: Split Now?

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: January 12, 2020

Nathan Schwandt is an Instagram star and gay YouTube personality who has a massive fan following on social media. He is a model and influencer on Instagram.

In addition to being a model, he is also a pet enthusiast who worked at a pet shop before his social media career took off.

Nathan Schwandt’s Personal Life: Split with Jeffree Star

Besides his career, Nathan’s dating life, too, is one of the reasons for his rise in stature. He has involved himself in a high-profile relationship with another internet personality, makeup sensation Jeffree Star. 

Nathan and Jeffree came in contact with each other through Instagram. They created a bond with one another before finally meeting in person. 

After a while of chatting and FaceTiming, Nathan flew out to Los Angeles to meet Jeffree. However, they didn't start dating right away.

The couple officially started going out in May of 2015, and since then, Nathan has appeared on many of Jeffree’s YouTube videos. 

Things were looking to have gotten serious between the two as Jeffree’s cosmetic company, Jeffree Star Cosmetics, launched his “The Star Family” collection in October of 2017, in which there were shades named after Nathan and their dogs.

The collection sold well, and Jeffree used the income from the sales to buy a house in Grand Rapids, Michigan, for themselves and Nathan’s family to share.

It seemed as if time passed by too quickly as the pair celebrated their third anniversary by posting many pictures of themselves on the 29th of March 2018.

In July 2019, Jeffree even surprised Nathan by buying him his favorite car, an Aston Martin Vantage in Lime Essence, costing around $155K.

Nathan Schwandt with Jeffree Star in September 2019 (Photo: Jeffree Star's Instagram)

But, as December 2019 approached, some rumors started floating around, claiming that Nathan had broken up with Jeffree. However, Jeffree clarified during a podcast with another YouTuber Trisha Paytas that Nathan had only deleted his social media a few weeks earlier, and there was no split to talk about.

Amidst the breakup rumors, Nathan and Jeffree started their new year, 2020, together by uploading a video on YouTube showcasing their newly-acquired house.

However, all this seemed to be a facade to cover up their breakup for the longest time possible. On the 11th of January 2020, Jeffree confirmed their breakup through a video on his channel titled “We Broke Up.”
