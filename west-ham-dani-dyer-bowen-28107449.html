Jarrod Bowen 'has priorities sorted' as he chooses FIFA 23 over time with Dani Dyer

Jarrod Bowen has 'got his priorities sorted' after jumping straight on FIFA 23 over spending time with his girlfriend Dani Dyer.

The former Love Island contestant posted a video of the couple, back together after the winger returned from international duty with England. Well, together is used loosely with Bowen getting deep into the latest edition of FIFA this year.

The game is set to be released on 30 September but many have been granted early access to the game, including the vast majority of Premier League players. And on Dani's TikTok account, she posted a clip with the camera on herself - looking less than pleased - before turning to Bowen with a headset on paying his other half little attention.

“So excited to see my boyfriend,” she captioned the post. “But Fifa 23 had other plans for me.”

The video has already garnered over one million views and West Ham fans can only agree with Bowen's stance after coming home from England duty and jumping straight onto the game.

OddsBible posted: "Jarrod Bowen playing FIFA 23 instead of spending time with his girlfriend Dani Dyer… got his priorities sorted."

One West Ham fan replied: "Got to do what you got to do." A second supporter said: "Man of culture."

While another tweeted: "I see no issue here."

Bowen was called up to the international set-up last week, who is yet to score or assist this campaign in the Premier League. However, the 25-year-old did not play a single minute for the Three Lions.

The Hammers are sitting in the relegation zone after their tough start to the season. David Moyes' team have won just once this season and have four points, sitting two behind 17th-placed Wolves.
