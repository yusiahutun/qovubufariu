How to Get Up to Four Months of Amazon Music Unlimited for Free

With all music streaming services available, you've probably never been motivated to get an Amazon Music Unlimited subscription. Even if you have, Amazon raised subscription prices in August of 2023 for Prime members on the Individual and Family Plans.

But you now have the opportunity to try Amazon Music Unlimited without worrying about the cost. Amazon is offering it for free for up to four months for a limited time. Find out how to claim the offer below.

Who Is Eligible to Get Amazon Music Unlimited for Free?

The Amazon Music Unlimited deal is available to Prime members and non-Prime members who have never subscribed to Amazon Music Unlimited. Amazon already offers Prime members a one-month Amazon Music Unlimited free trial, but you can get an extra three months free through this deal.

Similar to Spotify, Amazon Music Unlimited boasts a catalog of 100 million songs you can listen to without any ads. You also get access to podcasts and other features like spatial audio and more.

Please note that, at the time of writing, the Amazon Music Unlimited deal is only available to users in a handful of regions, including the US, UK, France, and Germany.

How to Sign Up to Get the Amazon Music Unlimited Deal

Non-Prime members get three months of the Amazon Music Unlimited Individual Plan for free, while Prime members get a four-month free trial. Additionally, Prime members who already have an Amazon Music Unlimited subscription can upgrade to a Family Plan for free for four months. According to Amazon UK's page, the offer is available until October 11, 2023.

Here’s how to claim the free trial:

  • Go to the Amazon Music Unlimited page and click Try it free. (To get four months of the Family Plan for free, go to the Amazon Music Unlimited Family Plan promotional page and click Try it free).
  • Enter your login details (if you aren’t signed in), confirm your payment and delivery information, and click Sign-up and pay.
  • Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the process.

    • After the free trial ends, Amazon will bill you $10.99/month (for non-Prime members) or $9.99/month (for Prime members) unless you cancel beforehand. Family Plan subscribers will be billed $16.99/month.

    Once you’ve signed up, you can get the most out of the service by learning some essential Amazon Music Unlimited tips and tricks. And don't forget to set up a reminder to cancel the subscription, just in case you aren’t ready to make the financial commitment.

    Enjoy Amazon Music Unlimited for Free

    Streaming services don’t necessarily cost an arm and a leg, but the costs do add up with each monthly payment. Take advantage of the Amazon Music Unlimited free trial to get a feel of the service and decide if it’s worth your money. Lucky for you, you have three or four months to make a decision.
