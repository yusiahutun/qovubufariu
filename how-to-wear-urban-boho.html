How to Wear... Urban Boho

Media Platforms Design Team

I'm a bohemian at heart—fashion-wise at least. My closet is bursting with embroidered tops and dresses, both authentic ones I've collected from my travels and designers' almost seasonal interpretations of the summer staple, like this Suno blouse and skirt. While they are perfect for weekends in the city or trips to the countryside, I'm looking to take them to work (at least on days when a blazer isn't required.) I'm inspired by Donna Karan's spring show. It's proof that elegant jewelry, a sophisticated stacked heel and luxe bag can really transform boho pieces into an office-appropriate look, for a creative environment anyway. Bonus: The Neil Barrett heels will likely become your go-to summer shoe.

As Executive Editorial Director of HarpersBAZAAR.com, Joyann King has ushered America's first fashion magazine into an era of digital excellence. A decade overseeing the website, social media and video platforms, and special product launches like Bazaar Bride and digital covers has garnered her and her team critical recognition. In 2015, she was named one of Adweek’s New Publishers to Watch. King can be seen on ABC, NBC, FOX, and CBS discussing trends and the ever-changing relationship of technology and fashion. She was previously an editor at InStyle.com, ELLE.com, Glamour.com, and SELF.com. When she's not plugged in, the Texas native can be found relaxing with her family in upstate New York.
