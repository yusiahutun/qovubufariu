Nick Bosa Biography

Nick Bosa is best known as the Professional Football Player who has an estimated Net Worth of $33.5 million. Defensive end that made his NFL debut with the San Francisco 49ers in 2019 and was named the 2019 NFL Defensive Rookie of the Year. Previously, he was a unanimous All-Big Ten First Team and a Smith-Brown Big Ten Defensive Lineman of the Year as a sophomore at Ohio State University. He was born in the United States on October 23, 1997. In 2016, he was named ESPN Freshman All-American. He was a five-star recruit while playing high school soccer in St. Thomas Aquinas, Fort Lauderdale, Florida. He joined quarterback Jimmy Garoppolo as a key member of the 49er squad. His father, John, and his brother, Joey, both played in the National Football League. The sign of his zodiac is Scorpio.
