Britney Spears Says She Wore a Diamond Thong to Her Wedding

Britney Spears may have walked down the aisle during her wedding to Sam Asghari wearing a custom-made Versace piece, but she also had a certain risqué bridal accessory up her sleeve.

The pop star married her longtime boyfriend in an intimate, at-home wedding last Thursday. For the romantic celebration, she reportedly wore four different outfits: her off-the-shoulder Versace wedding gown, a black Versace blazer dress, a two-tone ensemble, and a red fringe minidress.

Now, according to a recent Instagram post in which she shared behind-the-scenes footage of her and Asghari's big day, Spears said she accessorized with unconventional bridal underwear.

"LIVING IS GIVING," she captioned a video that captured her dancing with her guests during her wedding reception. "Pssss don't worry I had my first diamond thong underneath my jacket … hope I didn't offend anyone 😜😜😜 !!!"

Spears previously reflected on the fairy-tale day via Instagram.

"Wow !!! Holy holy crap !!! WE DID IT !!! WE GOT MARRIED 👰🏼‍♀️," she wrote. "It was the most spectacular day !!! I was so nervous all morning but then at 2:00 pm it really hit me … WE’RE GETTING MARRIED 💍 !!! I had a panic attack and then got it together 🙄🙄 🙄."

The singer thanked the staff who helped transform her home into the perfect wedding venue, as well as gave a shout-out to her celebrity attendees.

"The crew who created our home into literally a dream castle were fantastic !!! The ceremony was a dream and the party was even better 🎉," she continued. "So many incredible people came to our wedding and I’m still in shock 😱 !!! @drewbarrymore my girl crush and @selenagomez who btw is way prettier in person if that’s possible both came !!! I was speechless … I kissed @madonna again 😉😉😉 and we danced 💃🏼 into the night with @ParisHilton."
