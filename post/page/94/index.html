LIVE Appoints Ross Patel As First Green Impact Consultant

published on August 19, 2024
LONDON, UK (vip-booking.com) – Live Music Industry Venues & Entertainment (LIVE), which represents 16 UK live music industry organizations, has announced the appointment of its first LIVE Green Impact Consultant. Working closely with LIVE CEO Jon Collins, Ross Patel will lead the initiatives of ‘LIVE Green,’ the organization’s expert group on sustainability issues, through facilitating cooperation across the organization’s membership while ensuring knowledge sharing and support can be provided to help sector-wide efforts in this area.
