20 of the Best Early 80s Songs

published on March 24, 2024
There is something about early ’80s songs that have the power to transport us to a simpler, more care-free and wild time: In 1981, the first space shuttle, Columbia, was launched, the Cold War was ending and MTV hit the air. By showing videos around the clock, MTV introduced a whole new way to experience popular music, and ultimately defined a generation. Those early ’80s songs began looking and sounding more vibrant: synthesizers became staples and genres from hip-hop to hair metal grew increasingly popular, while oh-so-cool fashion icons like Madonna and Cyndi Lauper left an indelible pop cultural impact.
