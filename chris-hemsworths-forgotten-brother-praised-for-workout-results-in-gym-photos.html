Chris Hemsworth's 'forgotten' brother praised for workout results in gym photos

It looks like Chris Hemsworth has some workout competition, as the “forgotten” Hemsworth brother Luke has shared ripped gym snaps.

To welcome in 2024, Luke treated followers to a glimpse of his abs on Instagram, and it sent them into a frenzy. But he isn’t the only one of the Hemsworth brothers to keep a good fitness routine. Chris, 40, frequently talks about keeping up with his athleticism, so it’s no surprise his siblings also go to the gym. The place in which Luke was exercising could have been the home gym the three brothers have called the Iron Temple. He has previously explained they work “incredibly hard” to stay fit.

Luke shares a gym snap to welcome in the New Year

The 43-year-old posted a topless black-and-white picture of himself in the gym, flexing his muscles as he looks down into the camera. The celebrity can be seen wearing a black pair of loungewear pants and is surrounded by gym equipment.

The gym shot was accompanied by the movie star relaxing as he smoldered for the camera, and the two pictures have followers hot under the collar.

Inside the Hemsworth ‘Iron Temple’

In a 2016 interview with Men’s Health, Luke Hemsworth explained he and his two older brothers, Chris and Liam, 33, have a home gym which they call the Iron Temple. But it’s not just the brothers who use it, nor is it always a peaceful experience.

“When Chris first bought his house out here, we set up the most incredible home gym in the world,” he explained. “When we moved I took the whole gym and now it’s all in my garage and it’s fantastic because we get Liam and, like, six people coming over and we work out together.

“Orlando Bloom was using it for a while, which was cool. It’s become another reason to hang out, and it makes training a lot easier when you’re getting pushed by each other—so that’s the wonderful part.”

The Hollywood star continued: “We’ve always been competitive. I yell at Liam all the time: ‘Move faster! Work harder!’ I’m like the trainer because I design all the workouts and implement and enforce them. Then on top of that, Liam and I are always surfing together, and Chris when he’s here.”
