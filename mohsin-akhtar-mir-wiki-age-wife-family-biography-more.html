Mohsin Akhtar Mir: Biography

He was born in the year 1974 in Kashmir, India. He used to do modelling for Manish Malhotra, who is a dear friend of Urmila Matondkar.

Mohsin Akhtar Mir: Family and wife

He was born and brought up in Kashmir and belongs to a islamic family. He also has a brother whose name is Muzammil Akhtar. He got married to the Urmila Matondkar, the Indian film actress on 3rd of March, 2016 and they both are living a happy life together.

Mohsin Akhtar Mir: Career

He began his career as a model. He modelled for the well-known fashion designer Manish Malhotra. He also shared the screen with Preity Zinta in an deodorant product advertisement in 2007.He made his acting debut with the film “Luck by Chance (2009).” He was the main lead of the movie “It’s a Man’s World (2009).”He also runs an embroidery business in Kashmir. He made his debut as a producer with a Marathi film “Madhuri.” in the year 2018.
