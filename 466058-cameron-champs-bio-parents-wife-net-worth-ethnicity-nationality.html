Champ was born on June 15, 1995, in Sacramento, California. The 27-year-old professional golfer is the son of Jeff and Lisa Champ. He comes from a bi-racial family with a black father and white mother. Jeff Champ and his wife owned a trophy business in Sacramento, California.
