KASS FM presenter Kimeto De Master has emotionally mourned his son who was recently murdered by thugs in Kirinyaga University as he was heading to the hostel.

Twenty-one-year-old Collins Kipchumba, a third-year student met his death a few days ago after he was accosted by alleged robbers while heading to the hostel and was hit with a blunt object.

A report by TV47 had indicated that the young student lost consciousness upon the attack and died a day later while receiving treatment at a hospital.

In a separate incident, a 19-year-old girl was fatally shot on June 13, on her way to the salon by trigger-happy police officers who were on a crackdown of the dreaded Confirm gang.

Whitney Atieno, who was accompanied by her friend 18-year-old Ruth Waithera, had gone to make her hair for school at a salon located 10 metres from her home in Lake View estate, Nakuru city.

A commotion happened just outside the salon and the two friends went to see what it was all about; that is where she met her death while Ruth was seriously injured.
