Is Missing: Dead or Alive on Netflix real? Everything you should know

On May 10 2023, Netflix released a true-crime series, Missing: Dead or Alive. It follows South Carolina police investigators working to solve the missing person cases of Amirah Watson, Lorraine Garcia, Sierra Stevens and David Taylor. The mysterious disappearances were quite shocking that many true-crime fans were left asking, "Is Missing: Dead or Alive on Netflix real?

The Netflix series Missing: Dead or Alive focuses on investigations of mysterious disappearances within the South Carolina police department. It features Smith, Jackson and Rains as they work in the ins and outs of each time-sensitive investigation and speak about how working on missing and kidnapping cases has impacted their lives.

Is Missing: Dead or Alive real or scripted?

Missing: Dead or Alive is real, and its four cases happened between 2019 and 2021. The series follows investigators JP Smith, Vicki Rains and Heidi Jackson, overseeing the entire Richland County Sherriff's unit. These officers work to solve the missing person cases of Amirah Watson, Lorraine Garcia, Sierra Stevens and David Taylor.

What happened to Lorraine Garcia?

The first Missing: Dead or Alive episode is about 61-year-old Lorraine Garcia, who disappeared in 2021. At first, her son Anthony, a war veteran with a PTSD diagnosis, was the main suspect.

During the investigation, neighbours reported that Lorraine often complained about her son's behaviour. Anthony could take her phone and bank card and lock her in the bedroom. Plus, Lorraine's home smelled like bleach, which made the investigators believe she might have been harmed.

Who abducted Amirah Watson?

In the second Missing: Dead or Alive episode, Amira Watson, 10, is reported missing by her dad, Mansoor Watson, in 2020.

JP Smith, the lead investigator of the case, suspects Amira Watson is likely with her mother, Tynesha Brooks and starts investigations. The U.S. Marshals took six weeks to locate Tynesha's family home in Georgia, where Amira was found.

David Taylor disappearance

David Taylor's case is the most exciting and mysterious in the true-crime series. Taylor's truck was discovered on the highway after winning the $10,000 lottery prize. The investigators assumed David Taylor was possibly attacked for the $10,000 prize money, but the ticket was never cashed in.

Was Sierra Stevens really missing?

Missing: Dead or Alive final episode was about Sierra Steven, a 17-year-old girl who went missing. In September 2021, Sierra Stevens slept over at a friend's house after a party without informing her foster parents.
