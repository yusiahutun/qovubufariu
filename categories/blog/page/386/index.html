House Of Blues Music Forward Foundation Launches 25th Anniversary Campaign

published on March 27, 2024
LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – The House of Blues Music Forward Foundation announced this week that it has launched a special campaign in celebration of the organization’s 25th anniversary year. As one of the nation’s top youth development organizations focused on the music industry, Music Forward has raised upwards of $25 million to support more than 1,000,000 youth since its launch in 1993. To honor this accomplishment and power future development, Music Forward’s anniversary campaign will run for 25 weeks and will be aimed at raising $250,000 to continue and expand impactful programs that accelerate opportunities for the next generation of music industry leaders and innovators.
