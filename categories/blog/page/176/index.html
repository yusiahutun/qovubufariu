Sky Sports F1 confirm major punditry shake-up ahead of Australian Grand Prix

published on July 08, 2024
Sky Sports F1 have made a big change to its Australian Grand Prix TV broadcast, halving the number of paddock pundits. For the next three races, starting this weekend Down Under, only four of the regular team will be in the paddock. The others will present from the broadcaster's main studio in Osterley, west London. Celine's First Fragrance Collection Includes 11 New Scents

published on July 07, 2024
If you've been waiting impatiently for a fragrance launch from Celine, the iconic French fashion house just made up for the wait—11 times over. The brand launched its first-ever "Haute Parfumerie" collection today, available on Celine.com and at the boutique on 650 Madison Avenue. Each of the 11 scents were inspired by memories from creative director Hedi Slimane's journal and curated under his artistic direction.
