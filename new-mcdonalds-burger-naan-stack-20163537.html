McDonald's new menu: Cheese sharebox, new burgers and a McFlurry launch tomorrow

McDonald's have completely revamped their menu, adding a new chicken burger among other things.

The fast-food chain are out with the old and in with new.

Today [September 24, 2019], McDonald's has removed the Double Quarter Pounder with Cheese, Spicy Chicken McNuggets and the Mars and Twix Mcflurry from the menu.

But don't stress, because the fast-food chain has introduced four new products in their place.

From September 25, 2019, which is tomorrow, McDonald's customers will have a load of new menu additions to try.

Including new chicken burgers and beef burgers.

As well as cheesy shareboxes an a "game-changing" McFlurry.

Here's what's on the new McDonald's menu...

The French Stack with Garlic Mayo

A returning favourite from last year.

The burger features two 100% British & Irish beef burgers with garlic mayo, cheese, bacon, seasonal lettuce and onions in a buttery garlic bun.

Price: £4.39 on its own or £5.99 for a meal.

The Indian Chicken in a Garlic Naan

A brand new and exciting product, the anticipation of the Indian Chicken in a Garlic Naan has sent fans wild.

The brand new chicken burger features two pieces of succulent crispy chicken with an Indian style relish, garlic mayo, lettuce, red onion and cucumber between two mini garlic naans.

Price: £4.19 on its own or £5.89 as part of a meal.

Cheese & Herb Melts shareboxes

The last time these were on the menu was over a year ago, but this time you can get Cheese and Herb Melts in sharebox form.

The Melts are made from a delicious blend of Emmental and Mozzarella cheese in a herb-infused breadcrumb and served with a rich tomato dip.

Price: £1.69 (5 pieces, one dip) or £4.49 for Sharing Cheese Sides (15 pieces, 3 dips)

Galaxy Salted Caramel McFlurry

McDonald's has launched a brand new flavour, Galaxy Salted Caramel McFlurry, which has never been seen in the fast-food chain before.

The new McFlurry is made from pieces of Galaxy chocolate and a swirl of salted caramel, all of which is then mixed into a soft serve ice-cream.

Price: 99p for a mini or £1.39 for a regular size.
