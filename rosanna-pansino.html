Rosanna Pansino Net Worth – $15 million

What is Rosanna Pansino’s net worth? Also, who is Rosanna Pansino’s boyfriend?

Introduction

Rosanna Pansino is an American author and YouTuber best known as the star of Nerdy Nummies, a web series that teaches people how to create “nerdy” treats like Disney’s Frozen and Super Mario cakes.

Biography

She was born on June 8, 1985, in Seattle, Washington, USA, as Rosanna Jeanne Reardon.

She is of Croatian, German, Irish, and Italian ancestry.

Her father, known as Papa Pizza, passed away in December 2019 due to leukemia. He has appeared in some of her YouTube videos.

”I am heartbroken. Late last night, my dad passed away,” Rosanna tweeted at the time. ”He had been fighting Leukemia for 6 years and refused to let his diagnosis stop him from living life his way.”

Rosanna has a younger sister named Molly.

In 2013, Pansino graduated from West Seattle High School. While there, Pansino participated in her high school’s drama club.

After high school, Rosanna went to Pacific Lutheran University.

”In college, I worked 4 jobs at a time to make sure I graduated with no student debt and did the same thing once I got to LA,” Pansino told HuffPost.

In 2010, Rosanna had a gig on the TV series Glee as one of the background Cheerio cheerleaders.

YouTube

On April 8, 2010, Pansino started her YouTube channel.

On June 17, 2011, Rosanna started the cooking show Nerdy Nummies, which is hosted on her YouTube channel.

For the series, Pansino bakes things like Pokemon cake pops, Lord of the Rings donuts, and more.

© Getty Images

As of March 2021, her channel has over 12.8 million subscribers.

”I really don’t focus on my success,” she said in a 2014 interview with Business Insider. ”All I care about is creating more fun content for my viewers.”

In 2012, she was voted “Queen of the Web.”

In February 2013, Pansino was nominated for a Shorty Award in Food.

Book

In November 2015, she penned her first cookbook – ”The Nerdy Nummies Cookbook.” In the book, Rosanna presents a collection of her favorite recipes, including – the Periodic Table of Cupcakes, Apple Pi Pie, Moon Phase Macarons, the Chocolate Chip Smart Cookie, Dinosaur Fossil Cake, and Volcano Cake.

”Creating a cookbook is something I’ve always thought would be a lot of fun,” Rossana confessed in December 2016. She added: ”Over the years I started to get hundreds and thousands of requests to do one. Each recipe has at least 6 step-by-step photos, with a total of over 420 full-color photos in the book.”

Also in 2015, Pansino released the song “Perfect Together” on iTunes.

In 2017, the YouTuber received the Taste Awards’ Foodie of the Year award.

© Getty Images

In October 2018, she released her second cookbook, entitled – ”Baking All Year Round.” In the book, Rosanna presents recipes for special occasions and holidays such as Father’s Day, Mother’s Day, Valentine’s Day, New Year’s, and Christmas.

In June 2019, Pansino was rushed to the emergency room for a rare bacterial infection.

Breast Implant Removal

In 2021, Rossana underwent breast implant removal surgery for the breast implants she got 13 years earlier.

”Surgery was a success! I’m so happy about going all-natural,” Pansino wrote on her Instagram. She also told her followers: ”While I don’t regret getting augmentation 13 years ago, I’m at a place in life where I’m just as happy without them!”

Baketopia

In 2021, she started to host Baketopia, a dessert competition series during which bakers create viral-worthy desserts for winning $10k. Baketopia is hosted on HBO Max. 

Boyfriend

Rosanna Pansino is in a relationship with Mike Lamond, a former sports commentator best known for his commentating on StarCraft II.

In May 2019, Lamond revealed that he is quitting esports casting due to damage to his vocal cords.

The two currently work on Nerdy Nummies.

So, how much is Rosanna Pansino worth?

Pansino earned most of her wealth from sponsors and ads on her popular YouTube channel.

Some of her sponsors are HUM Nutrition, Fleischmann’s® Yeast, Sensodyne, NordicTrack, and OGXBeauty. She also has a baking line with Wilton.

On YouTube, Rosanna has over 4.4 billion views, meaning about $13.2 million in revenue before taxes.

”When I first started making videos on YouTube, I didn’t have one cent to my name,” Rosanna told People at VidCon 2016. ”I was completely broke for four years.”

In 2016, she earned around $6 million.

Pansino also makes money from selling her books (Baking All Year Round and The Nerdy Nummies Cookbook) and selling merch on her website – rosannapansino.com.

Rosanna has her own show on HBO Max and has appeared in several TV series, including – Bad Internet, Emo Dad, A Heist with Markiplier, and more.

Pansino’s house in California is valued at $8.4 million. In December 2021, Rosanna bought her sister a house for $2 million.

Therefore, American YouTuber Rosanna Pansino has an estimated net worth of $15 million.
