Win a Billy Currington Limited Edition Vinyl + Letterpress Print Poster

The final Ram Jam singer has been announced, meaning the lineup for the invitation-only show in Nashville on Dec. 29, 2012 is locked down. Billy Currington will join previously-announced performers Zac Brown Band, Brantley Gilbert, Kellie Pickler and Easton Corbin on stage, and once again, you can enter to win a trip to the show as well as some free gear from Ram Jam and Currington. 

The singer shows off his new song 'Swimmin' in Sunshine' in the video below. Fans watch as a shirtless Currington pursues his other great love -- surfing. He says finding balance on a paddle board "relates to finding balance in life." He adds, "They go hand in hand. That's probably why surfing has been so good to me."
