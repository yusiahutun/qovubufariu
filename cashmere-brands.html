The best cashmere brands to invest in now

SHOP ALLUDE

Munich-based brand Allude has been crafting planet-conscious cashmere since 1993. The premise is simple: beautifully made, seasonless staples, made with consideration for the environment at every step.

The brand, which works from a head office powered entirely by green energy, offsets the carbon emissions of every sale and funds the replanting of mangrove trees in Madagascar via Eden Reforestation Projects (they’ve racked up 798 so far).

As for the pieces? They’re contemporary, chic and classic all at once: expect ribbed sleeveless tank tops (ideal for in-between weather days), on-point polo collars and versatile crew-neck sweaters.
