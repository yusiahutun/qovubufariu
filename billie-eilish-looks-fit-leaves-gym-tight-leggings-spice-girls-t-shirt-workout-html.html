Billie Eilish looks fit as she leaves gym in tight leggings and Spice Girls T-Shirt after workout

Billie Eilish appeared in good spirits on Tuesday as she flaunted her fit figure while leaving the gym in Los Angeles after a sweaty workout.

The 21-year-old pop star appeared makeup free behind clear glasses and wore a black Spice Girls T-shirt with tight grey leggings.

The beauty - who has 109M Instagram followers - tucked her long dark tresses under a black hat and wore a small cross necklace.

Eilish (whose real last name is O’Connell) had her arms full as she carried her cell phone and a refillable water bottle and was followed closely by her one-man security guard, who wore a black vest, cargo pants, and combat boots.

The seven-time Grammy winner wore black Moon Boot low-back snow boots and tucked her workout sneakers in her arm.
