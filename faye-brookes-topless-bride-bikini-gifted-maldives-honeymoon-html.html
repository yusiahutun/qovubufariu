Faye Brookes poses topless in 'bride' bikini bottoms as she shares snap from her gifted Maldives hon

Faye Brookes took to Instagram on Sunday, to share a snap from her gifted Maldives honeymoon.

The Coronation Street actress, 35, posed topless in 'bride' embellished bikini bottoms for the jaw-dropping shot.

Faye showed off an eyeful of her peachy behind in the attention-grabbing upload, and insisted in the caption alongside that she's 'smashing life as a wife.'

The soap star married husband Iwan Lewis in a picturesque Cotswolds ceremony, at the end of August.

The couple then jetted off to The Maldives on honeymoon, courtesy of Coco Giri Maldives and Blue Bay Travel, who Faye has repeatedly tagged on social media.
