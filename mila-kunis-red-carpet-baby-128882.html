Wow — Mila Kunis is one hot mama!

Ashton Kutcher‘s stunning wife recently hit the red carpet for the first time since giving birth to the couple’s second baby — and her post-pregnancy figure looked amazing!

During the Tuesday, March 28th appearance in Las Vegas to promote A Bad Mom’s Christmas in Las Vegas, Mila got candid about life with her two kids, Wyatt, 2, and Dimitri, three months.

Mila on March 28.

“It’s different,” she told STX Films chairman Adam Fogelson about being a mom to a young son and daughter, according to Us Weekly. “He’s also three-months-old, so you forget what sleepless nights are like. I remember, guys, in case you’re wondering.”

Mila, 33, and Ashton, 39, previously married in 2015 and welcomed Wyatt in October 2014 and Dimitri in November 2016.

In a previous interview, Mila revealed how parenthood has changed her life. “Everything is different,” she told Business Insider. “From the fact that you’re so happy when you get six hours of sleep to the idea of being responsible for this incredible, living breathing little human being that you can’t believe is yours.”

Ashton and Mila with Wyatt.

“[Motherhood] is the most life-changing experience you can have, in my opinion. Everything changed. I am proud to be a stay-at-home mom. I have no desire to be in front of the camera. I find [Wyatt] to be the most challenging job I’ve had,” she additionally said during a Reddit AMA.
