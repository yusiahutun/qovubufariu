Ellie Goulding reveals she got christened so she could wed Caspar Jopling

Ellie Goulding's wedding to Caspar Jopling was one of the most romantic spectacles of last year. The couple tied the knot at York Minster with famous faces taking to the pews for the ceremony, from Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom to Princess Eugenie and Jack Brooksbank.

Now the singer has revealed she needed to get christened before marrying the art dealer. "I was never brought up with any particular religion, whereas my husband was," she said. "So I did get christened so I could get married."

Goulding also opened up about her love life before meeting her now-husband. "I kissed quite a few frogs. That sounds harsh but that's the best way to say it," she confessed. "I did have a few relationships before Caspar... that weren't what I thought they were. But when I met Caspar, I just knew."

The couple began dating in March 2017 after reportedly being introduced by Princess Eugenie. The star was previously rumoured to have dated Prince Harry after the two were spotted at the Audi Polo Challenge in 2014. She also had relationships with McFly bassist Dougie Poynter and radio DJ Greg James.
