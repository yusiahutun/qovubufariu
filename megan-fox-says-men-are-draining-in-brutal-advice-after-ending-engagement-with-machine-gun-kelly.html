Megan Fox says 'men are draining' in brutal advice after ending engagement with Machine Gun Kelly

Megan Fox is sharing her advice for single women, shortly after confirming the end of her engagement with Machine Gun Kelly.

The celebrity couple became engaged in January 2022, as Machine Gun Kelly popped the question under a banyan tree. Cracks began to show in the relationship a year later, when Megan Fox suddenly unfollowed Kelly on Instagram, removing all their photos together. However, it looked like the two had kissed and made up shortly after, although the Hollywood actress seemingly confirmed the engagement was no more on the Call Her Daddy podcast.

Megan Fox advises single women to ‘not waste their time’ on men

Every celebrity was out at Coachella and/or Revolve Fest this weekend, including Megan Fox who showed off her stunning blue locks. The Jennifer’s Body star described the look as “cosmic and galactic” as she stunned in an Alexander Wang LBD.

Although the star was ‘not willing to explain’ her relationship status with MGK last month, she gave him a shout-out at the festival. “Obviously I’d have to say Machine Gun Kelly,” she told E! News while describing her dream Coachella line up.

She then went on to dish out her advice for ladies wanting to live their best life this ‘single summer.’ Although she did admit she wasn’t sure if she was the best person to give advice on the subject.

“My advice is just to learn a skill or develop a hobby, and do not waste your energy on boys,” she laughed. “All they’re gonna do is drain you, just move on. Invest in yourself,” she concluded.

Before meeting the Bloody Valentine singer, Megan was married to Brian Austin Green for 10 years.

On the Call Her Daddy podcast, the movie star admitted she never got to experience her “young single era”.

The star channels her inner Cowboy in feisty look

It looks like 2024 is the year of the Cowboy hat, and the Jennifer’s Body star may have won the look with one of her latest fashion looks.

The 37-year-old stunned in a straw hat with a blue jewel at the front, matching her colorful new hair. She wore a simple white tank top underneath a leather jacket, completing the look with some denim shorts and black boots.

“Summertime sadness,” she captioned the Instagram snap, which had fans going wild. “She’s looking like her younger self,” wrote one.

Another said: “The blue is going CRAZY.”

She also shocked fans by posting a pic in the look next to TikTok sensation Charli D’Amelio. A duo we never knew we needed!
