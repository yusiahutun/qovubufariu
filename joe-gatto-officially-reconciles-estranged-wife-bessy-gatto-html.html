Joe Gatto officially reconciles with estranged wife Bessy Gatto nearly two years after they split

Joe Gatto is off the market once again, reuniting with his old flame.

One year after the 47-year-old Impractical Jokers star announced that he had 'amicably' parted ways from his wife of eight years, Bessy Gatto, 41, the funnyman announced on his Instagram page that they were back together, as of Saturday.

Relaunching their relationship on his Instagram page with a cute photo of the pair posing together on a beach at sunset, Joe captioned the snap with, '10 years ago when we started out, I could have never imagined the absolute crazy ride with highs and lows our marriage has endured.'

He added, 'But there was always love. And me knowing that there is no one else I’d want to be my person. I love your compassion, your resilience and your giggle.'

Forgoing his usual comedy standup for all out seriousness, the producer continued with, 'Thanks for this decade. And I’m happy and hopeful that we will be able to get a few more in because we have proven with compassion, forgiveness and an open heart we can do this. Together.'

In the sunset snap, Bessy rocked a feminine pink dress and let her brunette locks flow loosely around her shoulders, as she held a glass of wine. 

Joe couldn't hide his huge grin as he kept his arm wrapped around his wife's shoulders, with the New York born actor sporting a simple white collared shirt and blue slacks. 

He kept gushing about his wife of nearly a decade in the caption, adding, 'Looking forward to more of it all, including laughing together, memory making with our incredible family and of course…dogs.' 

Finally drawing the lengthy, heartwarming caption to a close, Joe finished off his adoring post to his wife with, 'Love you so much Mrs. Gatto.'

The couple tied the knot back in 2013, three years after the brunette beauty packed her bags from her California home and moved across the country to be with Joe, her boyfriend at the time. 

Their first daughter, Milana Francis, followed two years later, with their son, Remington James being welcomed into the world in 2017, making him two years younger than Milana. 

Although the couple stated that the initial shock separation in 2021 was 'amicable' Bessy admitted in a TikTok following the separation that she felt 'broken.' 

After all, the self-described introvert had once captioned a post of herself with Joe on the red carpet with, 'Not much gets me out of my comfort zone and in heels.'

She joked that in spite of the fact that she typically didn't hit red carpets frequently, she was, 'full of pride and smiles being here,' to support his comedic husband.

Single no more: Fans of Impractical Jokers were understandably shocked when the couple announced their separation in 2021 given how frequently they gushed about each other on social media platforms 

The band's back together: Despite their estrangement, Joe and Bessy sparked reconciliation rumors several times during their split when they were spotted together taking their daughter to concerts like Imagine Dragons 

Significant date: The pair took their daughter out to see Imagine Dragons on Valentine's Day 2022 

Despite their sad split news, the couple just couldn't stay away from each other, particularly due to the fact that they were still parents to their daughter and son. 

They first sparked reconciliation rumors when they took Milana to her first concert, Imagine Dragons, on Valentine's Day 2022. 

Despite the fact that they were still broken up at the time, Joe and Bessy bought a new house together in April of this year, according to Life and Style. 

The pair never actually followed through with a divorce, meaning that their marriage is still intact.
