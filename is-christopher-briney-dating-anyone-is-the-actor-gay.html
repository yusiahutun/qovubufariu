Meet Actor Christopher Briney! His Dating, Gay, Height, Age, Net Worth

Christopher Briney is an up-and-coming actor and filmmaker who in recent times has stood out for his performance in the Prime Video’s series The Summer I Turned Pretty which debuted on the streaming platform on 17 June 2022.

The series is based on the first novel in the young adult romance trilogy The Summer I Turned Pretty by author Jenny Han.

Anyway, in the rest of the writing, find out about the character Christopher plays as well as some truth about his personal life, who he is dating, his sexual orientation, and more.

Christopher Briney On Prime Video’s The Summer I Turned Pretty

The Summer I Turned Pretty sees leading lady Lola Tung as its leading lady Belly Conklin, rounding out the love triangle with the Fishers brothers Conrad and Jeremiah played respectively by actors Christopher Briney and Gavin Casalegno.

Christopher’s Conrad “Con”/”Connie” Fisher is the brother Belly has always loved, and the summer we get to see her is the summer she finally catches his eye. As for Jeremiah, their feelings for each other start to flower, surprising them both. Albeit, whatever may happen in between, Conrad, according to the original story in the book, is supposed to have a happy ending with Belly marrying him and the couple starting a new life together.

If you also need to know, a week before the series debuted, Amazon had announced that its Season 2 would premiere sometime in Summer 2023.

Christopher Briney’s Net Worth

Christopher Briney reportedly had above $250K as his net worth as of 2022.

The Prime Video series is his first long-term role, so far. Albeit, it is not his debut in the industry altogether. He has directed and starred in several short features. Also, as of the time of this writing, he was set to appear in Dali Land opposite Ben Kingsley, Ezra Miller, and Rupert Graves.

What’s more, according to BIO on his website, he enjoys watching movies, photography, and pitching in the New York City Men’s Baseball League, when he is not in front of the camera.

The star in the making has also shared that it was not until he attended the Waterbury Arts Magnet School and serendipitously took an acting class that he discovered this his passion, his real call.

Is Christopher Briney Dating Anyone?

Christopher Briney was certainly this beautiful fellow actress Isabel Machado as of June 2022. And they both made sure to not keep this affair a secret.

Christopher had offered several glimpses of their romance on Instagram. From pictures of sharing kisses to Isabel sitting on his lap and every time they looked at one another with so much love, one could tell they are so much in love.

Also, on 23rd February 2022, when Isabel turned 24, the couple had the best of times.

Isabel also adequately featured Christopher on her Instagram @isabelrmachado. Apparently, Isabel also mentioned this account as a “baby and children’s clothing store”.

On Christopher’s account, Isabel also has gushed that he is the only reason that brings her to midtown or North Carolina.

IMDB credits Isabel (Rose) Machado as an actress for Akron (2015) and Minuet (2010). And apparently, like Christopher, she also studied BFA Acting for Film, Television, Voiceover & Commercial at Pace University. She likely also graduated in May 2020.

Is Christopher Briney Gay?

Now that Isabel is established as his girlfriend there is clearly no need for this discussion. Still, let us clarify that Christopher is not gay.

To those who gossiped about him being gay on grounds of him kissing Dan Kulesza, his fellow BFAian at Pace University, let us tell you that the guys kissed only for a school acting project. Even Isabel was a part of this project.

Dan, here, has worked as a production assistant for La Oposicion (2017), as per IMDB.

Christopher Briney Age

Christopher, with the birth name Christopher Thomas, was born in 1998. So, he reached the age of 24 in 2022.

Christopher Briney Family

Christopher’s father is Michael R. Briney who is from Tucson, Arizona. Also, according to Facebook, he worked at Thacher Proffitt & Wood according to his Facebook. He joined the company the same year he lost his beloved wife, Mrs. Kelly Briney. She passed on 10 May 2014 after a long battle with brain cancer and later in November Christopher started his new job.

Kelly, who lived till 58, was married to Michael for 27 years. They had first met in 1984 in a New York Opera Scenes Workshop. Two years later and they got married.

Late Kelly, among working different jobs, legal and administrative, was actually into singing and acting. She had a gorgeous soprano voice. But, more than her love of music and theatre was her devotion to her Catholic faith.

Kelly is also survived by Michelle Elizabeth Briney, her daughter, and Christopher’s sister. She was born on 13 February 1996.
