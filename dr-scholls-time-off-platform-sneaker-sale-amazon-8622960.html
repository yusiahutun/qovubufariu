Shoppers Can Walk 25,000 Steps in These Flight Attendant-Worn Sneakers

Traveling this summer? It’s time to start looking for the ‘It’ sneaker — and this year, it might be time to consider shoes that are going to be as good for your feet as they are for your outfits. Podiatrist-approved footwear usually doesn’t scream trendy, but there are exceptions, such as Klaw sneakers, Hoka slides and, of course, Dr. Scholl’s famous sandals. 

Dr. Scholl’s has kept our feet comfortable for decades, and has even managed to do the (seemingly) impossible — make orthopedic shoes cool. The Time-Off Sneakers fit the brand’s mold; they’re stylish with a shell toe and not-too-high platform, and they look good with everything from dresses to athleisure. The water-resistant kicks are made from recycled bottles, contain anti-microbial and anti-odor properties in the insole, and have the brand’s signature anatomical cushioning and arch support for superior comfort. They also happen to be 30 percent off on Amazon right now, so you can snag them for just $70. 

If all white isn’t your thing, the sneakers are available in a variety of cute colors like light blue canvas, silver foil, and even fun styles like a white floral fabric. They come in sizes 5 to 13, plus half sizes for a perfect fit, making it the rare sneaker that might actually look and feel good on everyone.  

If the sneakers look familiar to you, that’s because they’ve been going viral on TikTok for their comfort — not to mention, they’re Amazon’s best-selling women’s fashion sneakers right now. According to flight attendant and TikToker @allycase1, the Dr. Scholl’s Time-Off Sneakers are “the most comfortable white sneakers” she’s ever owned. As a world traveler, she walks a lot, “oftentimes tens of thousands of steps per day.” In fact, she said she loves them so much that she wears them almost every day whether traveling or at home. She’s not alone: TikTok has tons of examples of others jumping on the Dr. Scholl’s bandwagon, such as influencer @jacquelynmengel, who racked up hundreds of thousands of views on a video raving about how much she loves the sneakers. “I knew Dr. Scholl’s was known for making super comfortable shoes, I just didn’t know they were this cute,” she said about the “extremely comfortable” sneakers. 

More than 1,600 Amazon shoppers agree that this TikTok find is a real gem, with many noting how many miles they’ve walked in them while on vacation. “I wore these out of the box all day for 25,000 steps (about 10 miles),” one fan wrote, who added that they’re “just as comfortable” as their Hoka sneakers. One shopper said they’re “awesome,” especially if you have “wide or flat feet” but want to be “cute and stylish.” Another reviewer loved that they “don’t squish [their] toes together” and are “fairly easy to wipe down” to keep them fresh-looking. “I got these because I wanted a pair of comfortable walking shoes that weren't athletic looking for when I travel,” a third person wrote. “I can say after walking over 12,000 steps my feet did not feel tired or sore at all.”

A classic white sneaker that will keep your tootsies comfortable and looking good all day, and at 30 percent off? Sounds like it’s time to add a pair (or two) of Dr. Scholl’s Time-Off Sneakers to your cart before they sell out.
