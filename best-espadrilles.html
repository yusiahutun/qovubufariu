12 Espadrilles to Wear All Summer Long

The Summer Flat

Castañer Kampala 20mm Canvas Espadrilles

The Summer Flat

Castañer Kampala 20mm Canvas Espadrilles

As classic as they come, Castañer's slip on canvas espadrilles are made with 100% cotton and are a summer must. "I love the classic Castañer slip-on espadrilles for easy everyday wear," Davidson Hudson says. "Pair them with a white jean or a tailored short for a perfectly polished finished that you will want to default to daily!"

Size Range: 35-41 EU

Colors: Ivory

The Sandal Slide

Gucci Interlocking G Slide Sandals

The Sandal Slide

Gucci Interlocking G Slide Sandals

Gucci’s interpretation of the wardrobe mainstay is for those who like to show off a fresh pedicure. The interlocking G cutout strap brings a modern edge to the classic espadrille flat sole. Wear with a white shirtdress to work or a stripe tee and denim cutoffs on the weekend and finish off with a straw bag.

Size Range: 34-42 IT

Colors: Brown leather, metallic gold leather, orange leather and white leather

The Classic Wedge

Castañer Carina 60 Wedge Espadrilles

The Classic Wedge

Castañer Carina 60 Wedge Espadrilles

Another best-selling shape and editor favorite, Castañer’s lace-up wedge sandals are the slightly dressed up version of the heritage flat shoe. Wear them from a casual workplace to happy hour drinks and beyond. Often available in a wide range of colors each season, you can never go wrong with the tried and true navy, black or ivory.

Size Range: 35-42 IT

Colors: Navy, black, ivory

The Raffia Slip-On

Prada Raffia Espadrille Flat

The Raffia Slip-On

Prada Raffia Espadrille Flat

Prada’s beloved woven raffia style is definitely one to snap up quickly before it sells through like the popular raffia tote. The closed-toe design feels even more airy, thanks to a woven, fishnet overlay that will allow for breathability on the many hot days ahead. The brand’s infamous triangle logo gives this simple shoe the designer touch.

Size Range: 35-41 EU

Colors: Naturale

The Minimalist with Edge

Chloé Pema Espadrille-Sole Platform Sandals

The Minimalist with Edge

Chloé Pema Espadrille-Sole Platform Sandals

Now 55% Off

True to the house's DNA, Chloe's Pema shoe is an espadrille-inspired platform, reworked for the modern women with the brand's signature architectural detailing. A chunky jute heel is balanced with organic brass hardware, for a clash of textures that just feel right. Style yours with a billowy kaftan dress for an effortless hot weather look.

Size Range: 35-41

Colors: Black

The Bohemian Take

Altuzarra Kenda Espadrilles

The Bohemian Take

Altuzarra Kenda Espadrilles

Altuzarra’s shibori print finds a new home on the perfect summer shoe. Made in Spain, the reinforced rubber sole ensures no slipping while wearing. Take style notes from the designer and pair this slip-on with everything from warm-weather knit separates to stunning shirtdresses for an added pop. (And yes, it'll look just as good with a bikini, too).

Size Range: 35-41 EU

Colors: Butterscotch and berry blue

The Everyday Flatform

ALOHAS™ County Espadrilles

The Everyday Flatform

ALOHAS™ County Espadrilles

Now 14% Off

Buckle up with this flatform from Alohas that brings a new spin to an otherwise classic shape. Leather straps give the shoe a moto feel, especially in black, while a chunky espadrille sole adds a bit of height without being uncomfortable. The best part? They're made from sustainable leather.

Size Range: 5-12 US

The Laced-Up Wonder

Reformation Alessa Lace-Up Wedge Espadrille

The Laced-Up Wonder

Reformation Alessa Lace-Up Wedge Espadrille

Credit: Courtesy

Made for summer weddings and evening soirees, this lace-up shoe is as effortless as it gets. The most practical choice for outdoor gatherings, where stilettos might go straight through the grass, a wedge will provide lift and balance. Linen laces guarantee that these straps will hang tight around your ankle and leg, a must while dancing the night away.

Size Range: 5-11 US

Colors: Cream and black

The Perfect Platform

Alexandre Birman Vicky Platform Espadrille

The Perfect Platform

Alexandre Birman Vicky Platform Espadrille

Now 30% Off

If you are facing a long day of errands and appointments, strap into these platform sandals that will make hours on your feet a breeze. The flat shape is counterbalanced with a cushioned insole, so comfort does not fall to the wayside. The espadrille-inspired woven bottom proves that craft can be extremely cool.

The Modern Classic

Saint Laurent Paloma Espadrille Wedge Sandals

The Modern Classic

Saint Laurent Paloma Espadrille Wedge Sandals

What else would you expect from the brand that re-popularized the modern-day espadrille? Chic and simple, this wedge delivers seriously stylish results. A peep toe ankle strap shape in black complements the natural tone of the braided wedge beautifully. The subtle gold YSL logo charm on the ankle gives this shoe a little designer flair. Dress up or down for a versatile option that won't fall flat.

Size Range: 34-42 IT

Colors: Black

The Luxe Buy

Chanel Cotton Tweed & Grosgrain Espadrilles

The Luxe Buy

Chanel Cotton Tweed & Grosgrain Espadrilles

What is more iconic than Chanel tweed? The brand’s signature fabric makes for a fabulous update on the classic summer shoe. Complete with quilted stitching, a grosgrain toe, and the infamous double C logo, this is one luxe buy that will live in your closet for seasons to come. Wear with your favorite crisp linen shirts and light-weight denim for a relaxed feel.

Size Range: 34-42 FR available in select boutiques

Colors: Light yellow and black

The Party Starter

Tom Ford 140mm Laminated Leather Sandals

The Party Starter

Tom Ford 140mm Laminated Leather Sandals

Now 40% Off

"I am obsessed with Tom Ford's hyper-elevated take on the classic espadrille, rendered in a range of exciting colorways and finished with a dramatic ankle wrap. I think it's one of the best statement shoes of the season," Hudson Davidson tells BAZAAR. "It will work perfectly to dress up any to the more diaphanous-shaped summer dresses this season or to transition a classic jean from day-to-night wear." Play up the disco vibes with the gold color way that was made to boogie.

Size Range: 36-42 IT

Colors: Gold and pink
