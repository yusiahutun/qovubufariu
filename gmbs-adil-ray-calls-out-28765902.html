GMBs Adil Ray calls out co-host Charlotte Hawkins for embarrassing TV blunder

Good Morning Britain’s Adil Ray didn’t hold back this morning after he called out his glamorous co-host Charlotte Hawkins for touching up her flawless makeup during the five-minute advert break.

During Monday’s visit to the ITV studios saw the 48-year-old return to the helm of the breakfast show with the blonde bombshell as they discussed the latest news in the headlines.

One of the biggest stories on everyone’s lips was the ongoing strikes by doctors and nurses working for the NHS, but it didn’t take long before the show derailed slightly.

Attempting to brush over the problem, Charlotte joked: "I don't know what you're talking about" before she confessed one of her most embarrassing moments during her time on the TV programme.

She confessed: "I have to admit, once I was caught on camera doing it, I was like, 'Yeah, I've got ages' until you realise that you're in that wide shot."

In an effort to make up for the brutal swipe, the newsreader gushed: "I'm surprised you wear make up to be honest, I wouldn't have know [you were wearing any]."

To which Charlotte chimed in with: "Yeah, it's barely there, you know. I wake up like this, obviously not!"
