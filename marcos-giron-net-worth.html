Marcos Giron Net Worth

A professional tennis player from the United States named Marcos Andres Giron was born on July 24, 1993 (Spanish: Marcos Girón). On May 16, 2022, he reached a single career-high rating of No. 49, and on July 1, 2019, he reached doubles career-high ranking of No. 199. He won the men’s invitational in 2011 and the boys’ singles in the Ojai Tennis Tournament in 2009. Giron represented UCLA and won the NCAA Division I Tennis Championships singles title in 2014.

At the 2020 US Open, where he defeated Marc Polmans in five sets in his first match, and at the French Open for the first time in his career, Giron advanced to the second round of a major tournament.

Giron entered the Australian Open’s main event as a direct entry for the second year in a row but was defeated by sixth seed Alexander Zverev in the opening round in four sets.
