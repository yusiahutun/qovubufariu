James Charles 'making a meme out of himself' has people 'in tears'

James Charles “made a meme out of himself” with a striking Instagram selfie and people in the comments couldn’t spare their reactions. The snap sparked mixed thoughts and a number of social media users reacted with crying emojis.

The YouTube star and social media influencer has been a subject of controversy ever since he was hit with allegations, which he recently addressed and begged to be uncanceled. The beauty star is once again in the online spotlight after one of his recent Instagram posts attracted different reactions from people.

James Charles shares new Instagram carousel

James posted a dump of Instagram snaps featuring his weekend activities alongside the caption: “Happy Saturday.”

The YouTube beauty influencer shared a picture from Brentwood Farmer’s Market with a trendy tote bag, while a video saw him and a close friend unleashing their anger in a rage room.

However, it was the YouTuber’s last snap that distracted many netizens because it included a striking selfie with his back in the mirror which fully revealed his butt.

The last pic has people “in tears”

Many people were baffled over the relevance of the last picture in the Instagram carousel and the snap brought some people “to tears”.

“Last pic is crazy,” one person reacted. Someone else added alongside a crying emoji: “The last pic.”

Someone else brushed off the other comments and wrote: “Love how you make memes out of yourself. I would do the same tbh.”

“He let them out again,” another one noted as another follower reacted: “I [got] something in my eye.”

James launches his makeup brand soon

In other news, James is set to release his own makeup brand called Painted and he has been teasing the upcoming products with social media posts for weeks.

He announced the line of makeup products in April with a trailer on Twitter and Instagram and revealed that the brand took “four hard years” to come to life.

While the full details about the products are yet to be confirmed, James announced in a recent Instagram post that the line will officially launch in August.
