Pia Whitesell stuns in a $14,000 designer outfit at St. Tropez wedding

Pia Whitesell was a vision in black as she attended the wedding of Hollywood agent Ari Emanuel and fashion designer Sarah Staudinger in Saint-Tropez over the weekend.

Joined by her husband Patrick Whitesell, the executive chairman of entertainment conglomerate Endeavor, the 38-year-old turned heads in a $12,500 Valentino gown.

The one-shoulder frock, from Valentino's 2022 Rendez-Vous collection, revealed a glimpse of her toned torso thanks to its asymmetric cut-out at the waist.

Pia completed her look with a $1,700 Cartier Love ring and her diamond engagement ring, rumoured to be worth $1.9million.
