65 International Women's Day Quotes to Share in 2024

Sure, any time is the right time to salute and support the fairer sex, but some days were literally made for it—like International Women's Day! Since its official founding in 1911, people around the world have celebrated women's achievements and called for gender equality on the March 8th, United Nations-sanctioned holiday. There are loads of ways to get involved, including by patronizing women-owned businesses or hosting or attending a special function. Or, you can simply help boost the event's signal by sharing one of the following International Women's Day quotes on social media with the hashtags #IWD2024 and #InspireInclusion. These thoughtful, powerful and uplifting words by and about women come courtesy of everyone from political figures like former First Lady Michelle Obama to businesswomen including Coco Chanel, as well as actors, writers and musical performers.

Of course, you can also celebrate this most important occasion by getting together with your squad over some cocktails and a delish app, or just by cranking up some of the best songs by country music's leading ladies! No matter what you have planned (you might also want to catch a concert film from a strong, inspirational woman like Taylor or Queen Bey), make sure to spread the word about International Women's Day with one of the empowering quotes we've rounded up for you. Some are funny, some are famous, and some are even short enough for a quick text, but any would be great to share with world. After all, as Melinda Gates once said, "A woman with a voice is, by definition, a strong woman."
