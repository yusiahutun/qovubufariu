Pregnant Katherine Schwarzenegger Rides a Bike with Her Mom

News of Katherine Schwarzenegger's pregnancy broke in April 2020, and since then, the star has been spotted around Los Angeles.

While this will be Schwarzenegger's first child, her husband, Chris Pratt, shares a son called Jack with his ex-wife, Anna Faris.

Schwarzenegger was photographed on Saturday riding a bike alongside her mother, Maria Shriver. For the trip out, the writer wore a camouflage puffer jacket with black activewear underneath. She completed the look with a chic pair of sunglasses.

Clint Brewer Photography / BACKGRID

A source previously told People that the couple was keen to start a family soon after getting married. "She can’t believe how much her life has changed since meeting Chris," the source revealed. "He was very clear with his intentions from the start, and she has never felt so loved and safe. They both feel so blessed to have found each other, and they can’t wait to expand their family as soon as possible."

This week, Schwarzenegger posed for a family photo to celebrate her brother's graduation. The author's baby bump was visible in the picture, which was taken by her husband. She captioned the sweet snap, "Reposting because I’m SO proud of my baby brother CHRISTOPHER!! I can’t believe you’re done with college and officially an adult in the real world! You’re the best human and we are all so proud of all your hard work at University of Michigan! Congratulations! We all love you! ♥️ PHOTO CREDIT @prattprattpratt."
