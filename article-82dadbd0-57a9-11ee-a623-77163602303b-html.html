Wanda Jean Dunlap Jordan | Death Notices

Wanda Jean Dunlap Jordan, 86, of Houston, formerly of Laurel, died Sept. 8, 2023 in Houston. A funeral service will be Saturday, Sept. 23, at 2 p.m. in the chapel of Brown Mortuary with burial in Nora Davis Cemetery. A viewing will be 1 p.m. until service time. Brown Mortuary, Inc. is in charge of arrangements.
