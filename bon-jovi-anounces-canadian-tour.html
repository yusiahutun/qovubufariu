Bon Jovi Anounces Canadian Tour

TORONTO (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — BON JOVI announced October 9 that they’ll be returning to Canada this fall for a series of arena concerts in nine cities across the country. The LOST HIGHWAY TOUR will kick off Wednesday, November 14th in Montreal, Quebec and wrap Saturday, December 15th in Vancouver, British Columbia. The tour will mark the band’s first concerts in London, Winnipeg, Saskatoon and Vancouver in more than a decade.

The Grammy-winning group is widely known for their numerous hits, including 'It's My Life,' 'You Give Love a Bad Name,' 'Who Says You Can't Go Home' and 'Bed of Roses'. The group has managed to stay a highly succesful touring band in the ever changing landscape of the music industry. Lost Highway debuted number one on the Billobard charts in June.

Tour dates:

11/14 Montreal, QC Bell Centre
11/17 Ottawa, ON Scotiabank Bank Place
11/19 London, ON John Labatt Centre
12/06 Toronto, ON Air Canada Centre
12/09 Winnipeg, MB MTS Centre
12/10 Saskatoon, SK Credit Union Centre
12/12 Edmonton, AB Rexall Place
12/13 Calgary, AB Pengrowth Saddledome
12/15 Vancouver, BC GM Place

– CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
