Stylish Indonesian footwear manufacturer, Aggiomultimex, delivers on form and function

Putting one’s best foot forward becomes effortless when it is clad in footwear crafted by Aggiomultimex. The leading shoemaker showcases Indonesian artistry in creating high-end style and quality that inspires confidence in its customers to take that first step towards great journeys.

CEO Subagio Lembono has had a lengthy shoemaking history. From working as a shoe company employee to trading footwear, Lembono, along with brother-in-law and later Aggiomultimex chief finance director Bruno Saputan, built an empire that offers a full-range collection of high-quality footwear, from baby shoes to high heels and ski boots.

“As traders, we had no control over the manufacturing delays of our suppliers. We wanted to reduce risks for our customers and this drove us to establish Aggiomultimex – to secure production and guarantee on-time delivery,” Lembono says.

Aggiomultimex operates factories in Sidoarjo-Surabaya and Tigaraksa. The factories are equipped with European machinery, which promotes higher productivity and energy efficiency.

In an industry heavily influenced by changing seasons and evolving trends, extensive design and development capabilities keep Aggiomultimex ahead of the pack. The company also works closely with clients to craft innovative, stylish and functional footwear that is high in durability and wearability.

Its innovative spirit and focus on quality have made Aggiomultimex one of the trusted shoe manufacturers of leading brands such as Vibram, Lacoste, Prada, Dolce & Gabbana and Oakley, to name a few.

Along with high-end brands, Aggiomultimex manufactures its own shoes and markets them under the Aixaggio and Concave lines. The company has launched Concave in Britain and Australia and aims to position the home-grown brand on the global stage.

Bullish on its continued growth, Aggiomultimex is open to partnerships with companies seeking a reliable shoe manufacturer.

“Aggiomultimex is always open for business,” Lembono says. “We welcome companies that are looking for a partner that delivers on both form and function.”
