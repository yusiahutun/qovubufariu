Katie Holmes Sparkles at Crystal+Lucy Awards

Katie Holmes and Rose McGowan both donned MaxMara for the 2011 Crystal+Lucy Awards in Beverly Hills Thursday night.

Holmes opted for black and white separates, while McGowan's cream romper dress contrasted with her sun-kissed skin. Other guests included Elle Fanning (in a wispy floral dress), and champion figure skater Michelle Kwan (showing a lot of cleavage). Check out the photos below:

Despite having been acting for more than a decade, Holmes was honored with the MaxMara "Face of the Future" award. Annette Bening was presented the Crystal Award for Excellence in Film by her longtime friend Martin Sheen.
