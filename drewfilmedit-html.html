DrewFilmedIt - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube star and director who is best recognized for his DrewFilmedIt YouTube channel where he posts the rap music videos he has directed. He often uploads long-form podcast episodes to the same channel. His videos have earned him over 210,000 subscribers on the platform. 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in June 2017. 

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos is titled "YNW Melly - Freestyle (Classic)" and has received over 8 million views. He is president of 100K Management, a media company that specializes in creating content for artists as well as publishing and merchandising. His popular drewfilmedit Instagram features photos of him on set, as well as sporting some of the latest fashion trends and luxury lines of clothing.

Family Life

He was born and raised in the United States. He has lived in Saint Petersburg, Florida.

Associated With

He is president of 100K Management which represents artists such as Ynw Bslime, YNW Melly, and King Von.
