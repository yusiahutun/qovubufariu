Lee Hodges Bio, Age, Height, Parents, Wife, Net Worth, Wiki

Lee Hodges is an Alabama-born personality and a professional golf player. He was committed to playing for Ardmore High School for four years. Lee was honored with the Daily Preps Boys Golfer of the Year title in 2012 and 2013. He earned medals at the 2014 Alabama 4A State Championships and was recorded as one of the top-10 finishes at the 2014 Alabama State Amateur.

Early Life

Lee Hodges was born on 14th June 1995 and was brought up in Huntsville, Alabama. His parents are Jeff Hodges and Karen Tucker and maybe he is the only child in the family. Leff went to Ardmore High School, where he practiced and played for the school golf for four years. He resides in Athens, Alabama. He received his graduation degree in CS in Management from the University of Alabama. 

Lee Hodges Career

Lee Hodges played for Athens, AL, United States, and turned professional in 2018. He earned the 2015 Conference USA Freshman of the Year title and completed the team-best four top-five finishes, after which he was placed in third place at the 2015 C-USA Championship. He earned the title of First Team All-Conference USA and competed at the NCAA Championships finishing in 152nd position. In his presence, the team led to third place at the Marana Regional, giving an entry to NCAA Championships.

From the 28th to the 29th of September, he played at the Shoal Creek Invitational and was named Conference USA Golfer of the Week after he attained first place. During the season, he played for the overall 13 tournaments for the Blazers and had a finish with top-20 in nine events. At the season’s climax, he earned the Conference USA Commissioner’s Honor Roll membership.

Senior Season (2017 to 2018)

Professional Career

After receiving a degree in 2018 and leaving the University, he began with the Mackenzie Tour and entered the Korn Ferry Tour in 2019. After turning professional, he won his first winning in 2020 at the WinCo Foods Portland Open. Later he earned an invitation to the US Open.
