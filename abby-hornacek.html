Abby Hornacek is an American journalist, television host, and travel enthusiast. Abby began her career as a reporter for Fox Sports San Diego in 2016, and since then, she has worked as a sideline reporter and digital host for Fox Sports, covering various sports events. Abby is known for her love of travelling and has visited over 30 countries. She documents her travels on her social media accounts and website. Abby has also been involved in philanthropic work and volunteered for organizations such as Phoenix Suns Charities and The Boys and Girls Club.
