Emma Stone Glams Up Businesscore With a Patent Leather Miniskirt and Electric Stripes

Emma Stone means business.

The La La Land actor has had a busy week of accolades and events, attending the Palm Springs International Film Festival film awards, Variety’s Creative Impact Awards, and W Magazine’s Annual Best Performances Party. For each occasion, she put a spin on businesscore—and, for the latest, she went with a glam twist.

Presley Ann

Stone stepped out at Chateau Marmont in Los Angeles, California, looking so cool in a shiny patent leather dress shirt that featured black-and-yellow stripes of varying width and a plunging neckline, tucked into a glossy patent leather miniskirt, both from Louis Vuitton’s Spring/Summer 2024 Ready to Wear collection. She paired the lustrous ensemble with black pointed-toe stilettos—footwear suitable for both the office and a night of fun. Stone kept her jewelry minimal with a dainty chain choker and diamond hoop earrings, swept her gorgeous red hair back into a sophisticated updo, and glammed up with a strawberry pink lip, black winged eyeliner, and peachy pink blush.

The annual star-studded event, presented by Christian Louboutin, commenced Golden Globes weekend and acknowledged W Magazine’s “Best Performance Issue.” It saw the likes of Margot Robbie, Kaia Gerber, Greta Gerwig, Barry Keoghan, Nicholas Cage, Andrew Scott, William Dafoe, Theo James, and Jon Hamm.

Amy Sussman

Stone displayed masterful daytime to nighttime fashion earlier in the day, donning a long black blazer dress layered over a lovely sheer lace one, which peaked out at the hem and neckline. She cinched it all together at the waist with a black leather belt and paired with glossy pointed-toe slingbacks. On Thursday, she gave a classic suit some Hollywood flair, wearing a custom Louis Vuitton outfit composed of a silky white collared blouse with large crystal buttons, a silky black ribbon tie, matching pants, and strappy black heels. The crème de la crème, however, was a black and white robe that featured an abstract floral print and was adorned with white crystals.

Stone will continue her big awards week with the Golden Globes on Sunday. She is up for Best Performance by a Female Actor in a Motion Picture – Musical or Comedy for her role in Poor Things and Best Performance by a Female Actor in a Television Series – Drama.
