Top 20 black male models in 2020

Gone are the days where black male models got overlooked for those that have a fairer skin tone. Today, agencies around the world scout dark-skinned models from every corner as blacks have proven that they are as good as other races. Influential fashion designers and houses like Gucci, Ralph Lauren, and Tommy Hilfiger are embracing diversity and including models of colour in their gigs.

To make it as a top black male model means that you have to be dedicated to your work and show how passionate you are. Some of the models have had to work twice as hard just to be noticed. The challenges they face, however, do not stop them from scaling to newer heights every day.

1. Broderick Hunter

Broderick Hunter is one of the famous black male models today. He first started modelling on Instagram before brands could pick him up. The model has worked with major designer houses like Ralph Lauren. He has also been featured in Ciara’s music videos and even got a role on HBO's Insecure.

2. Adonis Bosso

Adonis Bosso got into modelling after he accompanied an ex-girlfriend to a modelling agency. He was picked immediately and signed. From that time onwards, his star has never stopped shining. He had the opportunity to work with big brands like Dolce & Gabanna and Todd Snyder.

3. Staniel Ferreira

Staniel Ferreira is not only a favourite black male fashion model on runways and covers but also one to watch out for. It took him about five years to get signed to an established agency and since then, the hunk has never looked back.

Fante Prince’s long, dark, and healthy beard is one of the distinct features the model is known for. The chocolate skinned supermodel is eye candy to many females who adore him for his killer looks and amazing style.

15. Shaun Ross

Ross' albinism may have seemed like a barrier, but the young champion never let the opportunity to be a supermodel pass him. Kofi Siriboe is one of the fastest rising supermodels today. The Los Angeles born model has been in the modelling industry for about a decade. He has worked hard to ensure that he gets the top spot whenever there is a gig.
