Angelique Boyer's Beautiful Dating Affair; Turned Her Boyfriend Into Husband And Got Married Secretl

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: March 17, 2017

Your heart might ache when you see different stories regarding the separation of celebrities all over the tabloids, but some other celebrity couples can give you a new relationship goal.

In this context, we will be talking about Angelique Boyer’s grand affair with her handsome boyfriend, Sebastian Rulli. The pair who was able to steal hearts with their romantic chemistry in Teresa is now making people envy their love life.

Boyer Is Enjoying A Romantic Relationship:

The love birds Angelique Boyer and Sebastian Rulli first confirmed their affair on September 23, 2014, via Twitter. After making their relationship public, they have been publicizing their love life in the social media by sharing photos of them from their romantic gateways. In the picture, one can easily see the pair enjoying their time together.

On the occasion of Valentine’s Day, Angelique shared a video on her Instagram where she captioned-

“My beautiful love! Love without conditions, love to be and let be! Feeling in love and free every day. To love is to know that I can count on you and you with me! I love you so pretty Thank you, my life, for making me feel so much!@sebastianrulli”

Likewise, Angelique shared the image of them from their vacation in Dolphin Discovery Riviera Maya. 

There’s no doubt that they are happy together. But are they secretly married and are concealing their honeymoon like phase?

 Tied A Knot Secretly?

No doubt Angelique and Sebastian are pretty much in love and are spending quality time together but have they took their love story to a next level? Are they living like a husband and wife?

Well, there were many rumors about their wedding plans which stated that they will enjoy a secret wedding in the days to come. However, the couple hasn’t confirmed the news yet. The love story that started in “Lo que la vida me robo” seems to be fresh and heartwarming. Stalking them on social media, we find them dating but they don’t appear to be married. No wonder they will marry in the days to come.

French-Mexican actress Angelique Boyer is not only enjoying a blissful affair, she also has a great career which provides her immense fame and wonderful earnings. Popular for portraying the protagonist role in Teresa, Angelique enjoys the net worth of $6 million. Twenty-eight years old beauty is also popular for appearing in J-ok'el (2007), Abismo de passion (2012), Lo que la vida me robó (2013-2014), and Tres veces Ana (2016).
