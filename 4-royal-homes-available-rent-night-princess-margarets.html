4 royal homes available to rent for the night: from Princess Margarets Mustique villa featured in

Who would not want to stay in a monarchical mansion or princely palace, experiencing the kind of privileged and pampered life usually confined to the upper echelons of society? Well, no longer relegated to the fairy-tale fantasies of yore, it is now very possible to enjoy a stately sojourn of this most opulent nature.

From India to the Caribbean, many of the planet’s most impressive royal homes and dwellings have been made accessible to the public, not just as tourist attractions, but also as overnight accommodation. Take a look at four of the most stunning royal homes available to rent for a stay fit for a king or queen.

Lismore Castle, County Waterford, Ireland

Located within the verdant landscapes of the Blackwater Valley, this historic castle dates back to 1185 when the soon-to-be king of England, Prince John, initiated its construction.

After an illustrious history, today the fairy tale castle offers its extravagant halls as exclusive event venues and its beautifully appointed suites as accommodation for up to 27 overnight guests.

Stay highlights include bespoke fine dining, access to the grand gardens and estate grounds, and a host of enviable outdoor activities like fishing and golfing. Such charms were good enough for previous guests including Sir Walter Raleigh, Fred Astaire and John F. Kennedy.

Les Jolies Eaux, Mustique, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines

Les Jolies Eaux is a villa perched along the southern coastline of Mustique island, in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines. Once the holiday home of the late Princess Margaret, the reigning British monarch’s only sibling, the private Caribbean island abode was even featured in the hit Netflix series, The Crown.

The former royal residence now functions as an inviting rental, with five glorious en suite bedrooms that can accommodate 10 guests.

In addition to spectacular seaside living, the estate offers sumptuously catered beach picnics, adventurous outdoor island activities as well as luxury spa treatments.

Castello di San Fabiano, Tuscany, Italy

Famed for its rich culture, gastronomy and charming villages, Tuscany is one of Italy’s most romantic and historic regions. Nestled within its undulating hills, this family-run castle – once the property of the King of Naples – can now be hired on a bed-and-breakfast basis. Boasting several stately suites, the castle is ideally situated to explore some of the acclaimed Tuscan highlights.

Additionally, guests can partake in authentic Tuscan cuisine cooking classes, tours of the castle and its surrounds as well as the welcome opportunities to enjoy some of the region’s renowned viticultural delights.
