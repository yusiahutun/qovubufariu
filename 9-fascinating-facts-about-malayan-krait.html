The Deadly Venom

The Malayan Krait, also known as the Blue Krait or the Bungarus candidus, is a highly venomous snake native to Southeast Asia. Its venom contains neurotoxins that attack the nervous system, leading to paralysis, respiratory failure, and eventually death if left untreated.
