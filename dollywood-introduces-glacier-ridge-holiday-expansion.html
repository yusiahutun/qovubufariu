Dollywood Introduces Glacier Ridge Holiday Expansion

PIGEON FORGE, Tenn. (CelebrityAccess) Dolly Parton’s theme park in Pigeon Forge, Tenn., hasz opened its largest holiday expansion, the new Glacier Ridge.

Parton thanked workers for opening the expansion, which spans across the Timber Canyon, Wilderness Pass and Craftsman’s Valley areas of Dollywood.

“Christmas is so important to me for so many reasons,” Parton said. “I’m so thankful that it’s a time when our family comes together to share stories, remember old times, and make new memories we’ll take with us through the next year and beyond.”

The event lasts to Jan. 5.
