Alicia Keys Husband: Swizz Beatz Net Worth, Children, Wikipedia, Songs, Wife, IG, Real Name

Swizz Beatz, is an American record producer, rapper, and businessman from New York City.

At the age of 18, Swizz Beatz started to gain recognition in the hip hop industry through his family’s record label, Ruff Ryders Entertainment, as well as his friendship and work with East Coast rapper DMX. Working with DMX as his producer, DJ and hype man helped Swizz Beatz gain prominence as a high-profile personality in hip hop.

Swizz Beatz found a protégé in Philadelphia-based rapper Cassidy, whose success helped the launch of his own label imprint, Full Surface Records, in 1999.

Swizz Beatz went on to sign multiple artists to the label, including Eve, Mashonda and Bone Thugs-n-Harmony, among others.

Swizz Beatz Real Name

Swizz Beatz’s real name is Kasseem Daoud Dean.

Swizz Beatz Songs

Swizz Beatz went on to release two albums under the Fall surface Records label; the first was a compilation, titled Swizz Beatz Presents G.H.E.T.T.O. Stories, released in 2002, and later his debut studio album, One Man Band Man in 2007.

In November 2018, Swizz Beatz released his second album, Poison. Alongside fellow musician Timbaland, Swizz Beatz is known for creating the popular webcast series Verzuz in 2020.

Swizz Beatz Wife

Swizz Beatz has been married to Alicia Keys since 2010.

Swizz Beatz Children

Swizz Beatz and Alicia Keys have given birth to two sons; Egypt Daoud Dean born on October 14, 2010 and Genesis Ali Dean, born on December 27, 2014.

Swizz Beatz also has the following children; Prince Nasir Dean, Nicole Dean and Kasseem Dean, Jr. from his previous marriage with Mashonda.
