I Keep Wearing These Cozy Slippers That Are So Similar to Uggs

One thing about me: I’ll always opt for warm and cozy fashion pieces, and my footwear choices are no exception. That’s why I sported the same pair of run-down Ugg Tasman slippers years past their expiration date; they kept my toes toasty and were practically always sold out. The popular shoes were my ‘ol reliables — that is, until I met their newer, nearly identical little sister, the Koolaburra by Ugg Burree slip-ons, which are up to $40 cheaper. 

Koolaburra by Ugg Burree Slippers 

Made from another brand in the Ugg family, the slippers are practically impossible to differentiate from their pricier counterparts. They’re constructed with the same silhouette, style, and iconic suede upper, plus they’re exactly alike in their pillowy, plush feel — I can attest. Getting down to the nitty gritty, the only differences lie in the shoes’ material since the Burree pairs are made with faux fur lining and the original Tasmans have an authentic sheepskin interior. These lookalikes just dropped this month, so you’ll want to snag them at Amazon ASAP before they sell out. 

Koolaburra by Ugg Burree Platform Slippers

While you can’t go wrong with the classic Burree pair, I couldn’t help but snag the stylish platform rendition, myself. Comparable to the $130 Ugg Tazz Mules (which Gigi Hadid has worn), the Koolaburra slippers are $90 at Amazon. I’ve worn them daily since they arrived on my doorstep thanks to a super soft fleece interior that keeps my feet warm even on the chilliest of days. Plus, they’re easy to slip on for both quick trips to the mailbox and all-day wear. I’ve comfortably walked miles in my pair without issues; I seriously don’t think I’ve put on other shoes all season. 

Not only are the Burrees comfier than house slippers, but  they’re fashion-forward, too. I throw mine on with loungewear every chance I get, but I also style the pair with jeans and sweaters. Thanks to their low-profile silhouette, the slip-ons can be worn with practically anything in your wardrobe. Plus, they’re sold in a range of neutral colorways, so you can grab the perfect match for your outfit rotation. Personally, I snagged the black pair, but I’m already eyeing more colors now that they’ve become my new favorite shoes. 

Head to Amazon to shop the Koolaburra by Ugg Burree slippers before they sell out, and browse through more cozy styles from the brand, below.
