Steph Curry's Mother Shocks Jada Pinkett Smith By Simply Revealing Her Age

Celebrity By TooFab Staff | 5/4/2019 9:42 AM PT

"Pause right there for one second," says an incredulous Jada.

It takes a lot to catch Jada Pinkett Smith off guard, but Steph Curry's mother Sonya did just that while filming Monday's new "Red Table Talk" on Facebook Watch.

The basketball phenom's mom was joined by his wife Ayesha Curry, sister Syndel and sister-in-law-to-be Callie Rivers for a frank discussion about everything from anxiety issues to Steph's flirty fans ... but it was a simple revelation about Sonya's age that really shocked Jada.
