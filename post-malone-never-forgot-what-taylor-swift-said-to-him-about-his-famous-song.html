Post Malone never forgot what Taylor Swift said to him about his famous song

Post Malone has revealed that Taylor Swift complimented his song and he couldn’t quite believe it.

During an appearance on SiriusXM’s The Howard Stern Show yesterday, October 24, Post Malone, whose real name is Austin Post, spoke to the show’s host about the time that Taylor Swift complimented his music when they crossed paths.

Postie called the compliment “a really special thing”

“I was doing, I think like a radio show or something, and we were just passing by and she was like, ‘Oh my God, nice to see you. Like, Better Now, is f**king amazing,'” he said.

The music star explained how he responded, before complimenting her right back. “And I was like, ‘What? That’s f**king crazy. You’re a great f**king songwriter. Thank you so very much.

“There’s so many beautiful artists in the world and for another artist to acknowledge that is a really, really, really b**ching thing,” the 28-year-old said.

He also said that the 33-year-old was “genuinely one of the most kind and considerate” people he’d met and called her an “absolutely amazing’ songwriter.

Taylor is now a musician and a Halloween costume

This year we might see quite a few Taylor and Travis Kelce‘s on our streets during spooky season, as dressing as them seems to be a favorable choice.

Not only is it popular, but Travis has given his assent to the costume after commenting on a fan’s social media post saying: “[four laughing emojis] you’re the [goat emoji].”

The Instagram post in question featured user kidsuper dressed as Taylor in a red skirt and white top, while his friend sported a blue and beige two-piece.

The caption of the post read: “TMZ REPORTS ALL THE CELEBS ARE READY FOR TONIGHT!!! Best party of the year! Costumes mandatory!! See you all soon I’m ready to shake it off!!”

Protective boyfriend mode

The celebrity couple have been thrust into the spotlight over the past few weeks, what with Taylor attending her new beau’s NFL games, but now it seems he not only wants to be her boyfriend but bodyguard too.

He reportedly told one of her security to step aside and that he could take over after the pair went out to celebrate their surprise appearance on SNL.

An eyewitness reported: “Taylor and Travis seemed to really be having a great night and looked happy to be with each other.

“At one point, he actually told her security guard that he could step aside, like he’d take it from here.”

Talking about it later on his and his brother’s podcast, New Heights with Jason and Travis Kelce, he explained: “I feel like whenever I’m on a date, I’m always having the sense that I’m a man in the situation.

“I’m protective, yeah, for sure. You always have to have that feeling or self-awareness, I guess,” he said.
