Sol de Janeiro, Rare Beauty, &amp; More

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

To elder millennials like me, body sprays aren’t new. In middle school I lived for fragrance mists from Bath & Body Works and Victoria’s Secret. But the body sprays in 2024 are a major upgrade from the ones in the ’90s. Instead of just watered down perfume, these body mists are soft, light, and airy scents that are especially nice for every day.

The real difference between perfume and body sprays is the concentration of fragrance oils in comparison to the water and alcohol content is low, which is why the smell is on the mild side. This can be nice for those sensitive to strong fragrance oils, especially when applied directly to the skin. They’re also a more affordable way to fill your fragrance library with some fun new scents without spending your entire paycheck.

Body sprays and mists have gotten so popular in the past few months, there are 11 favorites we can’t get enough of. Shop them all from the comfort of your couch so you don’t have to elbow a tween for the Sol de Janeiro.

  • Rare Beauty Fragrance Mist

    Part of Selena Gomez’s new Find Comfort Collection, is the Rare Beauty Find Comfort Body & Hair Fragrance Mist with warm and cozy notes of sparkling lemon zest, jasmine, and cashmere wood. This one also contains skincare ingredients so you can safely apply it right to your body, such as niacinamide and ashwaganda extract.

  • Sol de Janeiro Fragrance Mist

    We’re not sure why tweens are obsessed with Sol de Janeiro body sprays but we don’t blame them. Every scent is great but Brazilian Crush Cheirosa ’40 Bom Dia Hair & Body Fragrance Mist is a new favorite. It features a unique take on florals with notes of black amber plum, vanilla woods, and jasmine blooms.

  • Henry Rose Body Spray

    Michelle Pfeiffer’s brand of eco-conscious and EWC-verified fine fragrances recently rolled out body spray that stands out on its own. The water-based and alcohol-free Henry Rose Blonde Barry Body Spray features succulent blackcurrant buds and juicy blackberry, blood orange, and nectarine wrapped with sensual musks and mineral woods.

  • Skylar Body Mist

    Vanilla scents are also back and better than ever. See Skylar’s Vanilla Sky Hair and Body Mist for example. It has warm and spicy notes of cappuccino, pure vanilla, and caramelized cedar.

  • Phlur Fragrance Mist

    When Phlur launched its Hair & Body Fragrance Mists, Sephora couldn’t keep them in stock. A standout is Mango Mood with the freshness of mango and the interesting addition of fresh orris flower, patchouli, vanilla, and brown sugar.

  • Bath & Body Works Fragrance Mist

    The original body spray is still going strong with new and improved formulas and scents, such as the Bath & Body Works You’re The One Fine Fragrance Mist. It has fragrance notes of white birch, velvety rose, and strawberry nectar, perfect for a night out.

  • Ellis Brooklyn Body Mist

    It’s hard to choose a favorite body mist from Ellis Brooklyn but we’re not surprised SUN Fragrance Body Mist is a best-seller. With fragrance notes of sweet clementine, orange flower water, and vanilla, it’s like summer in a bottle all year round.

  • Tom Ford Body Spray

    Tom Ford’s sexy Soleil Blanc scent also comes in an All Over Body Spray with notes of coco de mer, ylang ylang Comores Islands, and cardamom.

  • Moroccanoil Fragrance Mist

    With Moroccanoil being a haircare brand, of course its fragrance mist has ingredients good for your strands. In addition to a blend of spicy amber and sweet floral notes, there’s argan oil and vitamin E to soften hair and UV protection, too.

  • Jo Malone London Body Mist

    Fans of Jo Malone London’s English Pear & Freesia have another option when it comes to wearing the fresh scent. The English Pear & Freesia Body Mist has top notes of King William pear, middle notes of freesia, and a patchouli base.

  • Lush Body Spray

    Lush’s best-selling Body Spray is the cult-fave Twilight, with its cozy lavender and tonka absolute scent. It also contains glycerin to be soft and hydrating on the skin.
