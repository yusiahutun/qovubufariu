  • Nike enlisted world-beating Kenyan runners Eliud Kipchoge and Faith Kipyegon for a fashion runway in France

Are legendary Kenyan athletes Eliud Kipchoge and Faith Kipyegon fashion icons in the making?

Is Eliud Kipchoge a model?

Photos of world-beating athletes modelling Nike fashion wear at the iconic Palais Brongniart in Paris are turning heads on social media.

Kipchoge, considered the greatest marathoner in history, modelled a futuristic trench coat featuring puff shoulders.

According to some netizens, Kipchoge's gloss-black look, which he complimented with shades, gloves and baggy trousers, was straight out of Star Wars.

In 2020, Kipchoge was featured by GQ magazine in a stunning photoshoot boasting trendy wear worth over KSh 6 million, and the images made rounds online.

Kipchoge's fashion featured some of the world’s most luxurious brands, including Fendi, Gucci, Gorgio Armani, Dior, Ralph Lauren, Givenchy, Roches and Manolo Blahnik.

Among the trendy pieces he wore included a shirt and shorts from Fendi, which cost KSh 66,143 and KShh 93,414, respectively, Manolo Blahnik shoes worth KSh 83,443, Barton Perreira sunglasses, which cost KSh 43,558, and Gucci trousers priced at KSh 545,740.
