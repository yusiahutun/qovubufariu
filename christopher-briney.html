Christopher Briney - Bio, Age, Career, Height, Net Worth, Facts

Table of Biography

Christopher Briney is an American Television and Film Actor. Christopher Briney is predominantly recognized for his portrayal of ‘Conrad’ in “The Summer I Turned Pretty”. The series was very famous and this exposure has led to him amassing a social media fan base.

Early Life and Childhood

Christopher Briney was born on March 24, 1998, in Danbury, Connecticut, USA. He is 25 years old and is under the astrological sign, Aries. He holds American nationality.

His father’s name is Michael Briney who is a businessman and his mother’s name is Kelly Briney. He doesn’t have any siblings and is the only child of his parents. Moreover, he is a very secretive person and doesn’t talk much about his family and parents on social media as well as in other media as well. However, we know that he is very close to his parents loves them a lot, and wants to put them away from the media and the attention.

Christopher Briney – Education

Regarding Briney’s educational background and qualifications, he attended Waterbury Arts Magnet School for his basic studies. He then moved to New York to pursue his acting career. There, he went on to study Film, TV, Voice-over, and Commercial Acting at Pace University and completed his BFA.

Career and Professional Life

Christopher Briney began focusing on his acting profession after graduating from Pace University. He then was able to make his professional acting debut in a 2018 short film, “Under the Covers”. He also wrote a short film, “Paix” in the year 2019. After that, he went on to direct and edit the project as well. Then, in the year 2022, he was cast in a feature film, “Dali Land”.

Also, when he was studying at Pace University, he was able to work in Twelfth Night, Hamlet, Radium Girls, As Bees in Honey Drown, The Library Play, Pig Farm, and If We Were Birds theater productions.

Briney’s big breakthrough came when he was able to star in the series, “The Summer I Turned Pretty”. This TV show is based on the trilogy novel by Jenny Han who is an American author. Christopher was the main character in the series where he played the role of Conrad “Con”. The series went on to become a superhit due to which the actor became famous instantly.

Moreover, apart from acting, he is a filmmaker as well. His main hobbies are taking pictures as well as playing baseball.
