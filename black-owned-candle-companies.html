The 25 Best Black-Owned Candle Companies to Shop and Support

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Image courtesy of Ya Thamani

The Whitney Collection

Birnam Wood

The Whitney Collection

Birnam Wood

Credit: Amazon

The Whitney Collection's eco-friendly candles are the perfect finishing touch for a freshly cleansed space. Unwind as you relax near the fireplace with their birch, juniper, and cedar Birnam Wood scent, or transported to a backyard dinner in Tuscany with the amber, fig, and tuberose Garden Fig candle, among others.

Harlem Candle Company

22-Karat Gold Nightclub Map of Harlem "Savoy" Luxury Candle

Harlem Candle Company

22-Karat Gold Nightclub Map of Harlem "Savoy" Luxury Candle

Credit: Thirteen Lune

If you like luxury, vintage design, and excellent fragrances, then it's time to meet the Harlem Candle Company. Inspired by the art and culture of the Harlem Renaissance, these candles are almost too gorgeous to burn. Almost.

Forvr Mood

Left On Read Scented Candle

Forvr Mood

Left On Read Scented Candle

Credit: Forvr Mood

In the world of Jackie Aina, Left on Read is a delicious tropical affair, complete with amber, coconut, and sea salt. Your actual results might vary, but regardless, this candle will help you unwind any time of the year.

Sensual Candle Co.

Shai Candle

Sensual Candle Co.

Shai Candle

Credit: Thirteen Lune

Sensual Candle Co.'s signature wooden wicks turn into mini bonfires the moment you light them. Plus, each of the brand's rich and unique fragrance blends fill up an entire room. We have our eyes on the brand's Shai scent—it expertly combines soothing lavender with bright citrus for ultimate woosah.

Reisfields NYC

Classic Collection No. 4 Palo Santo + Sandalwood Candle

Reisfields NYC

Classic Collection No. 4 Palo Santo + Sandalwood Candle

Credit: Madewell

We'll be the first to admit that our candle stockpile isn't small, but tiny elevated details help make Reisfields NYC a stand out. While well-known for their dual-wick, recycled craftsman cement staples, this streamlined favorite from their classic collection puts a unique spin on the classic palo santo scent.

Terminal B

CDG - Paris Roses Musk

Terminal B

CDG - Paris Roses Musk

Credit: Terminal B

It’s easy to daydream about your next travel adventure after unwrapping Terminal B’s range of scented candles. The brand’s thoughtful packaging includes a candle bag, priority boarding pass with a scent description, along with curated scents that coordinate to travel destinations like Hawaii, Singapore, Paris, and Buenos Aires.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Ardor Bin

Lavender Moon Wood Wick Botanical Candle

Ardor Bin

Lavender Moon Wood Wick Botanical Candle

Credit: Ardor Bin

Ardor Bin's hand-poured candles are extremely earth-friendly, and contain crackling FSC-certified wooden wicks for that true campfire effect. Their minimalist aesthetic and fragrant note selections can fit any room with ease.

Bright Black

Koi Candle

Bright Black

Koi Candle

Credit: Bloomist

Bright Black aims to use their coconut and soy wax candles as a medium to share positive narratives about Black culture. This one in particular features a wooden wick and a blend of vibrant eucalyptus, hemp, and citrus notes that burn for up to 80 total hours.

Ya Thamani

SE23 Candle

Ya Thamani

SE23 Candle

Credit: Ya Thamani

Ya Thamani was concepted in London, England, and developed in Los Angeles with the intention of featuring a smaller selection of soy and coconut wax candles. In addition to the oak elm, tobacco and sandalwood-noted SE23 candle, the brand also offers an SE6 scent that combines olive wood, bergamot, and rose.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Cavo

Wash Day Candle

Cavo

Wash Day Candle

Credit: Cavo

The fun names of Cavo's soy wax candles make it easy to know exactly when to light them. Their Wash Day candle is scented with pineapple, mango, coconut milk, and a dash of sugar, for all of those tropical deep conditioners you've loved before.

Blk Sunflower

Pillow Talk Candle

Blk Sunflower

Pillow Talk Candle

Credit: Blk Sunflower

Blk Sunflower is based in Lakewood, Washington, and aims to provide their customers with clean burning candles made using coconut-soy wax, cotton wicks, and non-toxic dyes. Pillow Talk has an earthy, gourmand scent that burns for up to 65 hours.

Southern Elegance Candle Company

Large Mason Jar Candle: Signature Scents

Southern Elegance Candle Company

Large Mason Jar Candle: Signature Scents

Credit: Southern Elegance Candle Company

Add Southern charm to any space with these delectable mason jar candles made in North Carolina. The fantastic performance and cheeky candle names don’t miss. A few of our favorites include the peach- and champagne-noted I Love Jesus but I Cuss a Little, apple crisp-inspired Gimme’ Some Sugar, and pumpkin and cinnamon Fixin’ to.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Vela Negra

Ashé Candle

Vela Negra

Ashé Candle

Credit: Vela Negra

Vela Negra scents are inspired by Afro-Cuban and Guyanese heritages, with options like Ashé and Azúcar (a sugary sweet ode to salsa icon Celia Cruz) that make this candle line sparkle in a sea of options. Their signature black wax aims to dispel negative energies while supporting new beginnings. Plus, the crackle each candle's wooden wick delivers make for some serious ASMR.

BLK Essence

Sweetest Thing

BLK Essence

Sweetest Thing

Credit: BLK Essence

As BLK Essence puts it, their candle collection is a place where high-quality craftsmanship meets mindful living. It’s easy to see why fresh floral scents like Sunday Morning along with gourmand ones like the shea butter-, vanilla santal-, and coconut cream-noted Cuffing Season are fan favorites.

The Noble Brand

Magic Hour Soy Candle

The Noble Brand

Magic Hour Soy Candle

Credit: The Noble Brand

From heady mixes of rum, caramelized sugar, and vanilla to botanical aromas of green tea and lemongrass, you’re sure to find a thoughtfully crafted candle scent—and room mist, wax melt, incense, and reed diffuser––for any occasion or mood. Major plus: You can start off with a quick scent quiz online to find the right aromas to elevate your space.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Laguna Candles

Oud Bergamot Limited Edition Candle

Laguna Candles

Oud Bergamot Limited Edition Candle

Credit: Laguna Candles

Laguna Candles’s multi-colored ceramic collectables are wonderful statement pieces that also happen to make fabulous gifts. Select a signature home scent from options like Mahogany Palo Santo, Oud Bloom, Pumpkin Soufflé, with vessels that are a joy to reuse once your candles reaches their end.

The Black Home

Goddess

The Black Home

Goddess

Founder of The Black Home, Neffi Walker, applies the philosophy, "Life should be designed beautifully" to each of her creations, including this scented candle from her brand. Remember that line, “You a bad girl and your friends bad too,” from Beyoncé’s song,"Party"? This popular sandalwood, jasmine, and white tea candle quickly adds celebratory It Girl energy to your space.

Soul Food Candle Co.

Cocoa Butter Bby

Soul Food Candle Co.

Cocoa Butter Bby

Now 29% Off

Credit: Soul Food Candle Co.

Soul Food Candle Company is an Afro-Latinx company that expertly transitions cultural rituals into deliciously fragrant scented candles (though we don’t recommend tasting them). Along with aromatherapy-inspired scents, the brand also features a range of signature gourmand and earthy fragrances, like the jasmine and amber Cocoa Butter Bby, lavender, bergamot, and cedarwood Sandalwood Silked Waves, and more.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

CTOAN Co.

Onyx Plus-Size Pregnant Candle

CTOAN Co.

Onyx Plus-Size Pregnant Candle

Credit: CTOAN Co.

If you love decorative candles that double as works of art, CTOAN Co.’s celebration of the human form is a breath of fresh air. The hand crafted wax creations––thick thighs, belly rolls, back fat, dimpled bottoms, and more––are infused with good energy and are quite the conversation starters.

Reggie Hathorn Chicago

Joy Luxury Candle

Reggie Hathorn Chicago

Joy Luxury Candle

Credit: Reggie Hathorn Chicago

Each candle from founder Reggie Hathorn’s namesake brand is named after a phase of healing and growth. Entering your “selfish” era? Savoring all the good in your life? There’s a vessel for that. Along with the brand’s good vibrations, their candles’s sleek presentation and a fantastic range of scents will undoubtedly turn you into a repeat customer.

Martine Thompson is an artist and journalist who explores beauty, mental health, and self-care through a feminist lens.
