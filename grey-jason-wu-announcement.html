Jason Wu Just Introduced A New &quot;Sister&quot; Clothing Line On Instagram- Grey Jason Wu

It looks like grey is the new black for Jason Wu. The Taiwanese-Canadian designer shared news of his new "sister" clothing line, Grey Jason Wu, on Instagram this morning. 

The collection, named after his favorite color, is set to launch in June, and will be less expensive than Wu's main collection, WWD reports. The line's instagram, @greyjasonwu, has already posted some of the designer's sketches of the collection, including cool collared dresses, sleek culottes and an edgy moto jacket.  Check out the announcement below and click here to enter his #GREYLaVie world.
