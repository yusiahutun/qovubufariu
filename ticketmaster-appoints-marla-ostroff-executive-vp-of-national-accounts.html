Ticketmaster Appoints Marla Ostroff Executive VP of National Accounts

WEST HOLLYWOOD, CA (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Ticketmaster has appointed Marla Ostroff to the newly created position of executive vice president, National Accounts for Ticketmaster. She will be based in Ticketmaster's New York office. The announcement was made today by Irving Azoff, CEO of Ticketmaster Entertainment.


"Marla is a proven leader in this company and the industry," said Azoff. "It's with great delight that we welcome her back to Ticketmaster."


"Marla brings a passion and commitment for building lifelong client relationships where both Ticketmaster and our clients enjoy a win-win partnership that benefits both parties, and a love for Ticketmaster as a business and family that we all share," said Dave Butler, President of North American Operations, Ticketmaster.


"As a long time client we are thrilled to see Marla return to Ticketmaster and look forward to partnering with her for many years to come," said Nick Scandalios, Executive Vice President, Nederlander Organization.


Ostroff will lead a team dedicated to strengthening and supporting key business relationships with some of Ticketmaster's largest clients and partners including Madison Square Garden, Nederlander, the NBA, the NHL and more. In addition, she will work to drive strategic new growth opportunities for the company. As a 19-year Ticketmaster veteran, most recently as executive vice president for the Eastern Region, Ostoff returns uniquely prepared for her new role. She brings a wealth of knowledge and experience, as well as many key relationships at an executive level within the live entertainment industry, that will support Ticketmaster's continued path of innovation. –by CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
