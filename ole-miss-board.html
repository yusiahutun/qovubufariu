Ole Miss Limited Edition Serving Board Laurel Mercantile

  • Supports Scotsman Manufacturing Scholarship at Ole Miss
  • Made in Laurel, Mississippi
  • Limited edition
  • Made of a reclaimed Ole Miss maple court 
  • Ole Miss engraved on back 
  • Certificate of Authentication
  • Available in three sizes:
    • Small: 11" x 8" x 1"
    • Medium: 15" x 11" x 1"
    • Large: 20" x 16" x 1" (features routed handles)
  • Each board features a stripe from the painted section of court
  • Stripe chosen at random

Our limited edition Ole Miss serving boards are one of kind. Made of reclaimed court from the University of Mississippi, these boards reflect the ambitious spirit of Ole Miss. These limited edition serving boards feature a clean cutting surface with the branded Scotsman logo for daily use. The back displays the engraved and epoxy filled Ole Miss logo proudly alongside original nail holes from the floor.

Proceeds from the sale of each board will help fund a scholarship for students studying at the Haley Barbour Center for Manufacturing Excellence. Find out more about the scholarship here.

Never put your serving board in the dishwasher. The best way to care for this heirloom piece is with simple soap and water.
