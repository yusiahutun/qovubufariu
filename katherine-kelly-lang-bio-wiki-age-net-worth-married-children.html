Katherine Kelly Lang’s Bio, Wiki

Katherine Kelly Lang was born on July 25, 1961, in Hollywood, California. She is the daughter of Keith R. Wegman (father) and Judy Lang (mother). Her father is an American ski jumper who competed in the early 1950s. He also tied for 12th place in the individual large hill competition at the 1952 Winter Olympics in Oslo whereas, her mother is an actress. She graduated from Beverly Hills High School.
