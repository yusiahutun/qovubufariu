Daisy Jones &amp; The Six Features So Many Crop Tops

From a fictional closet stuffed with crop tops, this is the style Daisy pulls out the most often: bell-sleeved with a little tie at the front. You can imagine her rushing out to an open mic on the Strip, or to this party at Billy and Camila's house, scrambling to tie the shirt and find her keys. They're always worn with a bottom that has an extra high-rise, whether it's a denim short or a corduroy-like pant. The sleeves feel like an allusion to Daisy's onstage outfits when she's at her musical peak: They have the same flowy movement as her sparkly Halston caftans, made for raising her arms over her head and screaming the chorus of "Regret You" into her mic.

Daisy's most repeated styling hack is throwing an impractical but fabulous fur-trimmed coat over her shoulders.
