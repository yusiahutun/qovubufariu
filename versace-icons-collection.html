Versace launches Icons collection, a curated capsule of classics

The holy grail of excellent personal style is being able to get by on just a few hardworking pieces, curating a capsule wardrobe for yourself, which you can make work for any occasion, at any time of the year. This is the thinking behind Versace's latest collection, which is made up of luxurious wardrobe essentials that are designed to be a part of your wardrobe for decades.

Versace Icons is all about dressing extraordinarily every day. These are pieces that are simple yes, but exquisitely made, versatile and striking, all at the same time.

Courtesy of Versace

"I will wear every piece in this collection and have worn similar for decades," Donatella Versace said of the launch. "These luxurious wardrobe essentials fit perfectly and showcase the beautiful and powerful simplicity of our silhouette."

Courtesy of Versace

At the heart of the collection is a sculpted, boned, bustier, corset which creates the iconic hourglass Versace silhouette, and can be worn with everything from jeans to tailoring. This features alongside; a black fitted Grain de Poudre 'DV' trouser suit (Donatella's go-to uniform), a black jersey dress with Medusa ‘95 medallion detailing on the straps (inspired by the Atelier Versace collection of spring/summer 1995), a butter soft black leather blazer and the 'Greca Goddess' mini bag.

Courtesy of Versace

Courtesy of Versace

Many of these pieces are inspired by iconic Versace designs from the past, which adds to the timeless appeal of them, and they are all created to be building blocks of a great wardrobe.

Starring in the campaign is actress Anne Hathaway, and singer Chris Lee, who were shot by Mert & Marcus. "To me, the Versace Icons collection is timeless elegance with an edge," Hathaway said. "I’m so proud of these images which seem to represent Donatella’s vision of empowerment. What a thrill to be a Versace woman!"

The Versace Icons collection is available online and in stores from today.
