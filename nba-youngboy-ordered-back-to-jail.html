NBA Youngboy Ordered Back To Jail

(Atlantic Records)

LOUISIANA (CelebrityAccess) Rapper Youngboy Never Broke Again, aka NBA Youngboy, has been ordered back to jail while a state judge in Louisiana considers revoking his probation.

State District Judge Bonnie Jackson put the Baton Rouge rapper on probation for three years in August 2017 as part of a sentence for aggravated assault. Youngboy was required to refrain from all social media activity and the judge said she was 90 percent sure the 19-year-old rapper, real name Kentrell Gaulden, violated that requirement.

If so, Jackson said she plans to revoke Gaulden’s probation June 21.

Youngboy was recently involved in a shooting near the Trump International Beach Resort in Miami during the Rolling Loud festival. Gunmen fired at Gaulden and his associates on a street near the resort; Galuden’s associates returned fire and one bystander was killed.
