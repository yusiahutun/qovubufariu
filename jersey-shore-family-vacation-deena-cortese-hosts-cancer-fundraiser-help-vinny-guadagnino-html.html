Jersey Shore Family Vacation: Deena Cortese hosts cancer fundraiser with help of Vinny Guadagnino

Deena Cortese paid special tribute to her late father with a cancer fundraiser on Thursday's episode of Jersey Shore Family Vacation.

The 31-year-old reality star raised more than $40,000 at a Be The Match fundraiser at the popular Jenks Club in Point Pleasant, New Jersey.

'This is the best memory I've ever had with my roommates,' said pregnant Deena.

Special tribute: Deena Cortese hosted a cancer fundraiser in honor of her late father John Cortese on Thursday's episode of Jersey Shore Family Vacation

She noted that her father John Cortese who died in June 2016 of leukemia would be proud.

Vinny Guadagnino, 31, also threw his support behind the fundraiser following the death of his beloved Uncle Sal Giaimo of cancer.

Vinny served as auctioneer during a live auction at the event that included bidding to touch the hair of Paul 'DJ Pauly D' DelVecchio Jr, 38.

Snooki took nine shots of booze in a row with the top bidders and raised $2,400.

'Snooki is the MVP of drinking. It's like shooting a free throw with Michael Jordan, like boxing with Floyd Mayweather, swimming with Michael Phelps,' commented Vinny.
