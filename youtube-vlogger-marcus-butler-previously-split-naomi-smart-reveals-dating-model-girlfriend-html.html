Marcus Butler Dating Status Now; Meet His Model Girlfriend

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: March 04, 2020

Marcus Butler is a YouTuber who ran the channel, MarcusButlerTV. He primarily made vlogs and had amassed over 4.15 million subscribers.

Initially, Marcus started his YouTube, creating music and video mixes, and sports footages. He was still in college at that time. He also ran a secondary channel called Just Marcus, which has over 1.8 million subscribers. 

As of now, Marcus has deleted all contents from his channels, and the reason behind it is still unknown.

Girlfriend

The YouTuber is dating model Stefanie Giesinger. The two first met in May 2016 at the amfAr Gala in French Rivera. Shortly after, rumors about the two started surfacing.

Marcus Butler with girlfriend Stefanie Giesinger in February 2020 (Photo: Marcus Butler’s Instagram)

Marcus confirmed that they were seeing each other in September of 2016 by sharing their pictures and videos from a romantic trip to Mykonos.

About Stefanie Giesinger

She first rose to fame by making her TV debut on the reality TV show, Germany’s Next Topmodel, in 2014. She was a student in a high school in Rittersberg at the time.

Stefanie won the show and is one of the best known German models. As of March 2020, she has over 3.8 million followers on her Instagram.

Marcus Butler’s Previous Relationship

Prior to dating Stefanie, Marcus was in a relationship with Niomi Smart, a fashion and lifestyle blogger, and fellow YouTuber. The two started dating in 2013 and broke up in December of 2015.

Marcus Butler and Niomi Smart were once dating and termed “Youtube’s golden couple” (Photo Credit: dailymail.co.uk)

He announced their split via a YouTube video titled ‘What’s Going On?’ Marcus said the following in the video:

I'm sure a lot of you are aware, that me and Niomi, over the last month to six weeks haven't been spending a lot of time together publicly and also privately.

As of right now, Niomi and I… we're not together. We've both found separate places to live. I found mine already and am in my new place… Niomi is moving very soon. 

The last month to two months have been very tough for both of us. Obviously, going through something in a personal relationship is a very hard thing to do. 

Niomi also released similar statements through her social media.
