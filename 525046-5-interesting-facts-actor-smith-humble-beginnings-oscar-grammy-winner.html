5 Interesting Facts About US Actor Will Smith, From Humble Beginnings to Oscar and Grammy Winner

  • US actor Will Smith has amassed so much success in Hollywood and is now considered a global superstar
  • The Men In Black actor is regarded as one of the greatest actors currently, with his movies totalling KSh 450 billion
  • These are five interesting facts about the actor whose name has been dominating headlines of late

Will Smith's name is on everybody's lips because of recent revelations made by his estranged wife, Jada Pinkett Smith.

We take a look at some interesting facts about the versatile US actor.

Is Will Smith a Grammy winner?

According to X user @Psyche_Albert, in 1988, Will Smith walked away with three Grammys, bringing his total to four.

What is Will Smith's success?

Some netizens consider the Men In Black actor as one of the greatest actors currently.

He became a millionaire at 18 and was already a platinum-selling artist.

How Fresh Prince Of Bel-Air saved Will Smith

Some of his most notable work is on the 90s sitcom Fresh Prince Of Bel-Air. According to the X blogger, Will was facing bankruptcy, but when he scored the acting gig in his 20s, he was able to pay off his debt and still amass more money to top up his net worth.

When did Will Smith and Jada Pinkett separate?

People Magazine interviewed Jada Pinkett, and she revealed that they silently separated in 2016.

“We’re still figuring it out. We’ve been doing some really heavy-duty work together. We just got a deep love for each other, and we are going to figure out what that looks like for us."
