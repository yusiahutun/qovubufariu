E'Twaun Donte Moore (born February 25, 1989) is an American professional basketball player for the Phoenix Suns of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He was drafted by the Boston Celtics in the 2011 NBA draft and also played for the Orlando Magic after playing college basketball at Purdue University. In high school, he led East Chicago's Central High School to an IHSAA state championship.
