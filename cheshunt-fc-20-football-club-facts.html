The History of Cheshunt FC

Cheshunt FC is a professional football club based in Cheshunt, Hertfordshire, England. It was founded in 1880 and has a rich history spanning over a century.

The Club’s Nickname

Cheshunt FC is affectionately known as “The Ambers” due to the team’s traditional amber-colored home kit.

Cheshunt FC’s Home Ground

The club plays its home matches at Theobalds Lane, a stadium located in Cheshunt. Theobalds Lane has a seating capacity of over 3,000 spectators.
