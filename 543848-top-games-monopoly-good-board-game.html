Top 10 games like Monopoly GO that are as good as the board game

Monopoly GO has long held a special place in many gamers' hearts. Its blend of strategy, chance, and ruthless property acquisition has entertained generations. But did you know there is a world beyond the familiar Monopoly GO board game? Many games like Monopoly GO offer features like dice roll, fortunes rise, and cunning deals.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

Monopoly GO is an exciting mobile game that lets you play Monopoly with friends, family, and other players online. The game lets you discover boards themed after world-famous cities, fantastical lands, and imaginative locales.

The more properties you own, the higher the rent you will earn from fellow tycoons. But if you have played this classic board game for a long time, you may wonder whether other games like Monopoly GO are worth playing alone or with your friends.

10 games like Monopoly GO

Imagine the thrill of engaging in fierce economic battles, negotiating deals, and outsmarting your opponents, all while experiencing the same rush of excitement that Monopoly GO offers. Here are games like Monopoly GO that are as good as the board game.:

1. Bingo Tycoon

Bingo Tycoon is one of the best game alternatives to Monopoly GO. The game offers standard and special rules, fun daubers, and custom bingo balls and allows players to enjoy live bingo with multiple bingo cards.

The game has features like treasure chests, property development, and bingo tournaments and is free to play but offers in-app purchases. Bingo Tycoon and Monopoly GO are similar because of elements in Monopoly GO, such as rolling the dice and buying properties.

2. Business and Friends

Business and Friends is a free app that allows players to become businessmen and sell properties and lands to earn money and buy more properties and lands.

The game is equivalent to Monopoly GO because it is fun and challenging. It can be played online and offline with friends and family. Business and Friends is designed to test players' brains and memory, making it an engaging and entertaining experience for all ages.

3. Carcassonne

Carcassonne is a turn-based strategy video game based on the popular board game of the same name. It mirrors the mechanics of the actual board game where each player is granted seven followers (often called meeples).

Players take turns placing random land tiles on the board. Carcassonne is an alternative to Monopoly GO because it involves placing tiles to build cities, roads, monasteries, and fields and placing followers in those locations to gain points.

The concept is similar to Monopoly GO, where players move around a virtual game board, buy properties, and earn money by charging rent to other players.

4. Dice Dreams

Dice Dreams is a free-to-play mobile game and one of the best games similar to Monopoly GO for Android and iOS platforms. It combines board games, social interaction, and resource management elements into a captivating experience.

Players roll dice to explore a kingdom, collect coins, attack rivals, and build structures. Dice Dreams aims to rebuild kingdoms, each consisting of multiple buildings and decorations that can be constructed and upgraded using coins.

The game resembles Monopoly GO in that players roll dice to move around a board, collect resources, and build structures.

5. Europoly

Europoly is a video game based on the classic board game Monopoly but with a European twist. It is designed for 2-4 players and allows them to traverse Europe, buying properties, making deals, acquiring monopolies, building houses, and forcing opponents into bankruptcy.

6. Let’s Get Rich

Let's Get Rich is a casual game that is an adaptation of the classic board game Monopoly. It allows players to enjoy a similar experience on the Internet against their friends. The gameplay is almost identical to Monopoly, except players buy cities worldwide instead of streets.

Players roll the dice to move over the board, and if they land in someone else's city, they must pay for their stay. The game offers a set of fun characters instead of the classic Monopoly pieces, and players can use special cards to change the course of the game.

7. Monopoly Solitaire

Monopoly Solitaire is a card game that combines the classic gameplay of Solitaire with the iconic board game Monopoly. It is a single-player game where you play Solitaire to earn Monopoly Bucks and progress along the Monopoly board.

The objective is to collect properties, build houses and hotels, and become the richest landlord on the board. The game features endless solitaire levels, Chance cards, and the opportunity to unlock favourite tokens from the original Monopoly board game.

8. Monopoly Tycoon

Monopoly Tycoon is a construction and management simulation game, and it is one of the best games like Monopoly GO for Android. The difference, however, is that instead of using dice, it relies on the speed and innovativeness of the players.

The player operates a business that owns stores and apartments in a city derived from the Monopoly board game. The game can be played in single-player mode, where the player competes against AI opponents, or in multiplayer mode, where players compete against other online players.

9. Scythe

Scythe is a board game in which players represent fallen leaders attempting to restore their honour and lead their factions to power in Eastern Europe.

The game is an engine-building, asymmetric, competitive board game that involves conquering territory, enlisting recruits, reaping resources, gaining villagers, building structures, and activating powerful mechs.

These aspects of the game are almost similar to those of Monopoly GO. Scythe uses a streamlined action-selection mechanism to keep gameplay moving briskly and reduce downtime.

10. Ticket to Ride

Ticket to Ride is a turn-based strategy video game based on the popular German-style board game of the same name. The game allows players to build railway networks, claim routes, and compete against each other or AI opponents.

It is one of the best free games like Monopoly GO, where players collect train cards and claim routes on the map, earning points for each route they claim. The two games are similar because they feature simple mechanics, fast-paced gameplay, and great presentation.

Is Board Kings like Monopoly GO?

Board Kings and Monopoly GO share similarities but have distinct features. In Board Kings, players roll dice to move around a colourful board, collecting coins, cards, and other rewards. The goal is to expand your board, build structures, and visit other players' boards.

On the other hand, Monopoly GO is a digital adaptation of the classic board game Monopoly. Players roll dice, buy properties, and aim to bankrupt opponents.

Final word

The top ten games like Monopoly GO that are as good as the board game include Bingo Tycoon, Business and Friends, Carcassonne, Dice Dreams, Europoly, Let’s Get Rich, Monopoly Solitaire, Monopoly Tycoon, Scythe, and Ticket to Ride. These games offer diverse experiences, from strategic challenges to casual fun.
