What to Look For in an iPhone SE Case in 2024

Finding a protective iPhone SE case is surprisingly challenging. Not only do you need to make sure you're shopping for the correct SE model, but you'll need to wade through hundreds of products from dozens of manufacturers---some of which aren't built to withstand significant impact.

If you know where you're looking, however, there are plenty of great options. The easiest way to find a good iPhone SE case is to stick with long-standing manufacturers such as ZAGG, Spigen, and OtterBox. These companies have been producing high-quality cases for years, and you can be certain that their lineup of iPhone SE cases is built to the same standards.

You'll also want to consider how much protection you need for your new iPhone. If you're prone to dropping your phone or standing up with it on your lap, you'd be best served with something that covers all ports and buttons. If you need it to fit in a pocket for ease of use, opting for a slim fit or clear case is a better choice. Be sure to think carefully about how (and where) you plan on using your iPhone before springing for a case.

Once you've figured out your basic needs, we've put together a list of the best iPhone SE cases below. Whether you're looking for a case that doubles as a wallet, something with a bit of flair, or a case that encloses your phone like a fortress, here are a few great products to consider.

Best iPhone SE Case Overall: iPhone SE Silicone Case

Pros

Cons

✗ Not meant for rugged use

✓ Available in four colors

✓ Durable and non-slip silicone construction

Nobody knows the iPhone SE better than Apple, so it should come as no surprise that the tech giant makes one of the best cases on the market. Offered in four colors (Chalk Pink, Abyss Blue, Midnight, and Product Red), the Silicone Case perfectly contours your iPhone SE, providing a slim profile while still protecting your new device from drops and scratches.

Unlike most products in the Apple catalog, the Silicone Case is actually rather affordable, clocking in at just $35. However, despite its low price tag, no corners were cut in its production.

Built from a soft-touch silicone that's compatible with wireless charging, the case covers most edges of the iPhone SE, with a small cutout on the side for Ring/Silent toggle and a larger cutout on the bottom for its various ports and speakers.

Because of its slim profile and various cutouts, Apple's Silicone Case isn't meant for rugged use. So if you're planning on some wild excursions or have some precarious hobbies, you might want to consider something with a bit more support.

Everyone else, however, will find an affordable, well-designed product that is more than capable of keeping your phone safe during daily use.
