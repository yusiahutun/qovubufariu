Golden Globes: JLo wears '$500k' worth of jewelry which could buy her two Ferraris

The 81st Annual Golden Globes provided a night to remember, and so did JLo with her show-stopping jewelry, worth thousands of dollars. The 51-year-old stunned on the Red Carpet solo, although she was joined by her husband, Ben Affleck for the prestigious ceremony.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck made their Golden Globes debut as a couple, after reuniting in 2021, after over 15 years apart. Although The Gone Girl actor was nominated for an Award, his wife, and her outfit, stole the show.

Jennifer Lopez ‘supports Barbie’ on the Red Carpet

It was JLo’s gorgeous pink dress that had all eyes on her at the awards show. The On The Floor star stunned in a Barbie pink dress, custom-made by Nicole + Felicia Couture.

The strapless gown hugged the star’s toned figure, which came with a cape, trailing along the carpet. The sleeve area was detailed with roses to give the look one final pop.

Finishing off her ‘Barbie girl’ look, Lopez held a shimmery pink clutch purse, matching the jewelry that broke the bank!

A closer look at the star’s stunning Golden Globe jewelry – totaling $490k

Lopez accessorized with jewelry from luxury French Jeweller, Boucheron, including a huge pink Hummingbird ring, diamond drop earrings, and a diamond bracelet to match.

You only need to take one look to know that these don’t come cheap, and Diamond experts at UK retailer Steven Stone have taken a closer look to estimate how much they may be worth.

The 34-carat morganite ring is said to retail at £122,000 ($155,233). The earrings, set with 434 round cut diamonds, 52 round cut pink sapphires, and four round cut blue sapphires, total up to an estimated £138,420 ($176,126).

Last, but certainly not least, her Plume de Paon feather detailed bracelet, featuring 745 round cut diamonds retails at £138,420 ($176,116).

In total JLo’s bling totals up to an estimated just under £385k ($490k) worth of jewels from the luxury jeweler.

Maxwell Stone points out her first engagement ring from Ben Affleck in 2002 “boasted a 6.10ct radiant cut pink diamond,” Lopez’s favorite color.

Now, if the celebrity wanted to splash out on vehicles instead of gemstones, she could buy two Ferrari Roma’s at around $243k, or one impressive Ferrari 812 GTS at a staggering $429,815.

Bennifer are ‘couple goals’ at the Golden Globes

As well as looking fly together at the ceremony, fans spotted a cute moment between Bennifer, going viral on X, formerly known as Twitter.

Although the Argo actor didn’t pick up a Globe this time, he took to the stage with Matt Damon to present the award for Best Director for a Motion Picture. The award was given to Christopher Nolan for his incredible work in Oppenheimer.

Eagle-eyed fans spotted the adorable moment, where JLo could be seen proudly recording her husband as he took to the stage.

“JLo recording her man, Ben Affleck, is the cutest,” one user wrote on the platform, whilst sharing the video.

Couple goals!
