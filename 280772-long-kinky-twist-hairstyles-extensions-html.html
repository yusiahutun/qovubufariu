Long kinky twist hairstyles with extensions

Kinky hair is stealing the spotlight this year and what better way to follow this trend than with long strands. When it comes to rocking long kinky hair, the possibilities are endless, and in each style that you spot, you are bound to look stunning! Whether you want large flat twists, Mohawk-inspired hairstyles to super long styles with bright pops of color; these kinky twist hairstyles will keep you looking great for the next few months.

Not only are these hairstyles protective, but they are also low maintenance, easy to manage and great for withstanding any harsh weather conditions.

Over the last few years, long twist hairstyles extension has become increasingly popular. When done correctly, this protective hairstyle will aid natural hair growth and keep you looking amazing for many weeks. And the best part is that it is versatile, meaning you can style your long twists in so many different ways. For inspiration, here are some head turning, top, long kinky twist hairstyles ideas you can use for your next look.

1. Long dreadlock twist updo

Black women have been blessed with kinky hair that allows the hairstylist to be creative with the hairstyle. One of those hairstyles that you can slay with kinky hair is a flat twist updo that uses locs. Flat twist updo with extensions prevents bulkiness of the hair, offers protection, add length and gives your natural hair and scalp a few weeks to relax. You can opt to design your flat twist to go diagonally or in a zigzag manner and secure the hair ends using a pin or a cute flower hair band.

2. Make your kinky hair pop with ombre color

If you’re bored with wearing your hair in the same black shade then why not change things up a bit by opting for kinky twist extensions in ombre color. Ombre hair is a trend that is not fading out anytime soon. It works great in any hair texture but looks even more amazing on long kinky hair. A slightly different way of doing this is to add the pop of ombre color using hair extensions. The hair extensions provide much less hassle of wearing long hair which is great for beginners or those who lack long hair.

5. Bantu knots for ladies with long kinky hair that is irregular

Bantu knots are perfect for covering up short hair strands at the front without the need to trim your whole hair. The hairdo also works to give long kinky twist hairstyles an edge.

6. Cute twist singles hairstyles

If you don’t want to put too much stress on your hair at the front, then opt for a natural twist hairstyle with extensions that keep the twist at the back. Use clips, pins or natural hair elastics to hold the twist in place. 7. Easy ponytail updo Twist hairstyles for kids

Leaving the twists hanging loose is a great style for kids. However, a simple easy to do ponytail is one of the best twist hairstyles for kids.
