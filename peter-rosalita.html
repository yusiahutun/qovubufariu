Peter Rosalita is a professional singer, a cute little child from the Philippines who set the bar extremely high on the 16th season premiere of “America’s Got Talent.” He surprised all judges and the audience with his phenomenal performance. With his amazing and mesmerizing singing, Peter absolutely nailed the performance and advances to the semifinal.

Peter Rosalita Career & AGT

Peter entered his school’s talent show when he was six years old and placed third, according to the magazine. He started singing at the age of six and also attended music school to enhance his knowledge about music and master his skills in singing. He fell for music even before he could sing properly. Peter began singing in local competitions throughout the United Arab Emirates before branching out. According to The Global Filipino Magazine, Peter participated in talent searches in Abu Dhabi and Dubai from 2018 to 2021. Then he broadened his horizons by competing in other singing competitions held all over the world, from Almaty, Kazakhstan to Bahrain in the Middle East.
