1. InkaPelis

InkaPelis is one of the famous free Spanish movie sites where users can access content without registering or subscribing for anything. Among its top features is the provision for users to choose the video quality they want to watch from 480p to 720p. Another cool thing is that you can increase or decrease the playback speed to a comfortable level.

While it is not advisable to download content from websites or sources you do not know whether they are legit or not, this website does offer its users a download option for offline viewing. Its homepage layout is easy to navigate, with the most popular movies displayed there and a search box for other films on the site.

2. RePelis

RePelis has probably the largest selection of free movies in Spanish. However, its homepage has a different feel from those of other sites on this list. Among the standout features is a 'quick options' section where movies are sorted based on most recent, most voted, most-watched, latest updates, movies in theatres and DVD releases. In addition, a section dedicated to genre lists allows you to click a link for your favourite genre.

MegaDede is one of the best free Spanish movie sites because of its large selection of movies and its ease of use.
