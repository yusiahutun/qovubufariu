Devon Sawa and his wife Dawni welcome their second child, a little girl

He stole millions of young female hearts when he magically transformed from a ghost into a real boy to dance with Kat, played by Christina Ricci, in 1995's Casper.

Now Devon Sawa and his producer wife Dawni have more pretty magical news.

On Monday, they welcomed their second child, Scarlett Heleena Sawa, to the world, according to People.

Scroll down for video 

Sleeping soundly: On Tuesday, proud papa Devon Sawa took to Twitter to share this adorable snap of his baby daughter, Scarlett Heleena, who was born on Monday, saying, 'My wife's a rockstar'

She weighed in at a healthy 8lbs and 3oz, and measured 21 inches long.

The adorable newborn is a little sister for the couple's two-year-old son, Hudson.

Delighted Devon shared the news about the latest addition to his family with a snap of the sleeping tot he posted on Twitter on Tuesday, captioned: 'My wife's a rockstar.'

Family snap: Devon's producer wife, Dawni, has been documenting her pregnancy on Instagram and posted this snap of the couple with their two-year-old son, Hudson, just two weeks ago

Cute kisses: The 37-year-old actor is hoping Scarlett will be quiet and calm, 'unlike my son, who would shoot himself out of a cannon if we let him'

He told People: 'My wife Dawni and I welcomed a beautiful little girl into the world — our daughter.

'Please say prayers that she is quiet and calm, unlike my son, who would shoot himself out of a cannon if we let him.'

The 37-year-old former Nikita star mostly takes to Twitter with news about his family.

He can't wait: Two days before the birth, Dawni posted this snap, saying, 'Everyday this little boy asks to see the baby, then gently holding my tummy he says in the sweetest voice, "Come home baby, it's big brother Hudson" '

But Dawni has been posting pictures on Instagram with regular updates about her pregnancy.

Just two days before she gave birth, she shared a sweet shot of her son playing, captioned: 'Everyday this little boy asks to see the baby, then gently holding my tummy he says in the sweetest voice, "Come home baby, it's big brother Hudson."

'There is nothing more in the world that makes my heart feel so full of love! I love you more than you'll ever know ❤️ Baby girl, we are ready for you!!'
