Selena Gomez posts and swiftly deletes 'risky pic' replacing it with wholesome 'natural' selfie

Selena Gomez stunned the internet as she left little to the imagination as she showed off her curves in a new Instagram selfie, but deleted it shortly after, swapping it for a pure ‘natural’ selfie.

The music star is currently dating Benny Blanco and has no problems showing their PDA on the gram. However, this one selfie may have been a little too much for Selena Gomez, who quickly changed her mind and moved on.

Selena Gomez deletes ‘stunning’ selfie from Instagram and does complete 180

In the two now-deleted selfies, the singer could be seen with her hair in a voluminous side part, stunning in natural makeup with a hint of red lipstick.

The first snap posted was a close-up of her face, however, in the second, she showed off more of her curves as she posed in a black bandeau top, placing her hand behind her head. Luckily, she avoided any possible wardrobe malfunction!

However, it looked like the star quickly changed her mind and swiftly deleted the pics, although eager fans who were lucky enough to see the stunning selfies shared the screenshots on X, formerly known as Twitter.

“She is so so gorgeous,” wrote one fan.

“She has aged incredibly well. Good God,” wrote another.

After deleting the curvaceous selfies, Gomez then went for a completely different look, as she posted a bare-faced throwback selfie showing off all her freckles, with the caption “real”.

Friends including Camila Cabello and Nicola Peltz Beckham took to the post to admire their friend, as the former Fifth Harmony singer penned: “Good god woman have mercy.”

“You’re so beautiful,” wrote Nicola.

Wizards of Waverley Place reboot lands this Fall

The moment we’ve all been waiting for is getting closer, the reboot of Disney Channel’s Wizards of Waverly Place.

Many of us first got to know Gomez as Alex Russo, and she’s taking it back to where it all began. In an Instagram post, Disney announced the reboot would be coming this Fall, along with a then-and-now pic of Gomez and on-screen brother, David Henrie, who plays Justin Russo.

“Making magic, then and now. The new Wizards of Waverly Place series is coming this fall!” the caption read.

Sharing her excitement, the 31-year-old commented: “WE ARE BACK!! SOOOO GRATEFUL [heart eye emoji] MAGIC [wand emojis]”
