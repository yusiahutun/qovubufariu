NEW TATTOO! Vanessa Hudgens Gets Inked

Vanessa Hudgens got more than a front row seat for Fashion Week during her trip to NYC -- she also got a new tattoo!

Hudgens, who was spotted a shows for Anna Sui, Jeremy Scott and Marchesa this week, took time out of her fashion show viewing schedule to get inked!

Vanessa's bud, MTV Australia DJ Ruby Rose, shared the above photos on Twitter -- which document the "High School Musical" star getting a butterfly tattoo on the back of her neck by NYC tattoo artist BangBang.

After completing the tatt, BangBang tweeted "Vanessa! You rock girl! Enjoy!!!"

BangBang also did Rihanna's gun tattoo.
