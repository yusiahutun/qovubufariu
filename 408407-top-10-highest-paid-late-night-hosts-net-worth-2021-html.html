Top 10 highest paid late-night hosts and their net worth 2021

For decades, late-night shows have redefined social and political discourses in the world. They simplify news items, discuss culture, and, in some cases, unconventional topics. Besides the topics, most people watch the shows because of the hosts. Since the shows are personality-centred, all the highest paid late-night hosts have an edge when negotiating their TV networks' salaries.

Due to the massive demand for late-night shows, all major networks have special shows for this growing market. The trend has created many millionaires in the media space.
