How to wear it without looking frumpy

1Getty Images

There is a way of making the cardigan look sexy - pulls yours back over your shoulders like fashion influencer Alexandra Lapp to reveal your clavicle.

2Getty Images

Want to add an insouciant edge to an evening dress? Instead of turning to a leather jacket for a juxtaposition, choose an oversized cardigan instead. Allow it to fall off your shoulders in a nonchalant fashion.

3Getty Images

The closest you'll get to wearing a dressing gown outside, Gucci's midi-length knit cardi looks best when teamed with low-key denim.

4Getty Images

Forget a jacket, come spring, wear a slouchy cardigan over a simple T-shirt and jeans for a relaxed take on the trend.

5Getty Images

Style a fine knit cardigan with a midi-length skirt and boots, then accentuate the waist with an understated belt.

6Getty Images

Consider styling a slim-fitting cardigan with high-waisted cropped denim and kitten heels - styles in pastel shades lend themselves to the onset of spring.

7Getty Images

It is actually possible to make a cardigan work by night, as seen here on Alexa Chung - only a fine knit, slim-fitting cardi will do here. Tuck it into tailored trousers (or a leather style like Chung) for a pulled-together look.
