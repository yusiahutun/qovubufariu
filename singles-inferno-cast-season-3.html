Who Are The Netflix Dating Show Contestants?

It’s time to get back into Paradise and Inferno. The hit South Korean dating show is back and here’s everyone in the Single’s Inferno season 3 cast, so you don’t have to miss out on all the action.

Single’s Inferno debuted in 2021 and amassed a devoted following due to the shocking and dramatic nature of the show. 12 contestants are put on a deserted island called “Inferno” and compete through challenges to couple up. Once a desired couple is matched, they are sent to “Paradise” which is a fancy hotel where they could be able to get to know one another. Along with all the suspense and pairing, a set of panelists comment on the show including comedian Hong Jin-kyung, actress Lee Da-hee, Super Junior member Kyuhyun, rapper Hanhae, and YouTuber Dex.

However, season 3 is going to be way different from the previous 2. Instead of one Inferno, there will be two simultaneous Inferno islands with different contestants. “What used to be ‘Paradise’ and ‘Inferno’ will change completely,” Yoo Ki Hwan, Netflix’s unscripted lead for Korea, told Netflix Tudum. “Especially when it comes to ‘Paradise’ — it won’t just be a luxurious resort, but more of a special and intimate place where the couples have a chance to dive deeper into each other’s emotions, elevating the romance.”

So who’s in the Single’s Inferno season 3 cast? Read more to find out.

  • Kim Gyu-ri

    Kim Gyu-ri is the first contestant introduced in Single’s Inferno Season 3. She wants someone who makes her heart flutter and thinks of herself as a bubbly person. She’s looking for someone who makes her smile while looking at them. She’s revealed to be a professional model.

  • Choi Hye-seon

    Instagram: @hazelchoiii

    Choi Hye-seon is the second contestant we meet on Single’s Inferno season 3. She’s into manly men and does weight training at the gym frequently to the point that she’s known as “the human vitamin.” It’s revealed that she’s a Bioniformatics major at Ewha Womans University.

  • Choi Min-woo

    Instagram: @choimin_woo

    Choi Min-woo is the third contestant we meet on Single’s Inferno season 3. He’s into older women who have bubbly appearances. He thinks his best quality is his physique.

  • Lee Jin-seok

    Instagram: @seok_lj

    Lee Jinseok is the fourth contestant we meet on Singles Inferno season 3. He’s most confident about his chest and does weight training everyday of the week. He gets that he looks intimidating but finds that people find it endearing when he smiles. It’s revealed that he’s a bakery café owner.

  • An Min-young

    Instagram: @my_floria_

    An Min-young is the fifth contestant introduced in Singles Inferno Season 3. She thinks of herself as a happy-go-lucky person and will use her charm to fall in love. Minyoung is revealed to be a Pilates studio director and instructor.

  • Lee Gwanhee

    Instagram: @leegwanhee0429

    Lee Gwan-hee is the sixth person introduced in Single’s Inferno season 3. He’s a basketball player for the LG Sakers and thinks that he can get the girls on the first move. He doesn’t have any goals getting into the season but thinks it’ll be better than the last two seasons.

  • Son Won-ik

    Instagram: @hiwonik

    Son Won-ik is the seventh contestant introduced in Single’s Inferno 3. He’s been called an optimistic and has a boyish charm. He’s always open to new challenges and his goal is to find the perfect match in Inferno.

  • Yun Ha-bin

    Instagram: @yunhxvin

    Yun Ha-bin is the eighth contestant introduced on Single’s Inferno season 3. He thinks that his main charm is his eyes. He likes to push forward without giving up.

  • Yun Ha-jeong

    Instagram: @hi__jjeong2

    Yun Ja-heong is the ninth contestant to be revealed in Single’s Inferno season 3. She has a sly and foxy charm, but it disappears when she dates. She’s very popular and is super charming. She currently works in the clothing industry where she works for inventory, office work, customer service, and purchase orders.

  • Park Min-kyu

    Instagram: @ssrt_mk

    Park Min-kyu is the tenth contestant on Single’s Inferno season 3. His main charm is his arms and shoulders (He used to be called “Shoulder Beast” as a nickname). He’s revealed to be a South Korean police officer and a member of the Korean Coast Guard Special Sea Rescue Team.

  • Yu Si-eun

    Instagram: @yoo__si

    Yu Si-eun is the 11th contestant in Single’s Inferno season 3. In an instant, she reminds the judges of Jennie from BLACKPINK. She’s been popular throughout her life. She hears from people that she’s lovely, cute, and has a lot of enthusiasm. She’s a professional model and was a runner-up on Miss Korea.

  • Who are the Single's Inferno panelists?

    The Single’s Inferno panelists include comedian Hong Jin-kyung, actress Lee Da-hee, Super Junior member Kyuhyun, rapper Hanhae, and YouTuber Dex.

    Dex is a newcomer, having been a contestant on season 2 of Single’s Inferno. He told Netflix Tudum about his hopes for the new season. “I think the most important thing and what I’m looking forward to seeing the most is the level of genuine-ness the cast of Single’s Inferno 3 will bring. I hope we will feel like we are on the island alongside the cast and relate to the cast because of how well immersed they are on the island.”

    As for the advice he would give to the upcoming contestants, he said, “The most important thing would be to not lose yourself. I hope everyone is true to themselves, which is the most confident and cool way to appeal to their love interest rather than being someone you’re not. I wish everyone the best of luck!”
