50 Short and Sweet Inspirational Quotes to Get You Motivated

Whether you're in a bit of a funk you want to get out of, need to psych yourself up to meet a challenge, or are trying to persuade yourself to go for a new goal, sometimes you just need a good ol' heaping dose of inspiration. And while upbeat songs and funny movies can certainly help lift your mood, for real encouragement sure to stimulate you right down to your soul there's nothing like short inspirational quotes. Think about it: what better to help you get through times trying (and celebrate times triumphant!) than a few words of wisdom from legendary personalities renowned for their wise ways?

Below, we've gathered up quick quotes from leaders in politics, film, music, sports, science, literature and more, people who have sailed seas both smooth and stormy. They succinctly share the secrets to a successful life, touching on topics including individuality, diligence, courage ,and conviction. Plenty positive, self-affirming in the best way, and often just flat-out cheery, the following maxims come courtesy of everyone from Winston Churchill to Ruth Bader Ginsburg, Ralph Waldo Emerson to Rosa Parks. Jot down a few that especially resonate; pick one to be your new mantra—it just might help you change your life.

For as the great Dolly Parton says, "If you don't like the road you're walking, start paving another one!"
