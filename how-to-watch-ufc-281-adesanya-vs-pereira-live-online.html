How to Stream UFC 281 Live in the United States

UFC 281's main card, headlined by the middleweight title bout between Israel Adesanya and Alex Pereira, streams exclusively on ESPN+ in the United States. If you subscribe to ESPN+ ($9.99 per month or $99.99 per year) or the Disney Bundle (featuring ESPN+, Disney+, and Hulu's ad-supported tier for $13.99 per month), you can purchase the UFC 281 broadcast for an additional $74.99. New subscribers can get a year of ESPN+ along with UFC 281 for $124.98.
