Hailey Bieber, Sofia Richie, and More

Younger Hadid sister Bella is responsible for launching several trends in recent years (like the Adidas Sambas, clunky loafers with socks, layering vintage clothing, knit hats—the list goes on). When it comes to her casual off-duty looks, the supermodel doesn’t shy away from colors, patterns, or textures. She is often seen incorporating plaid, as well as other ’90s-inspired pieces such as printed sweaters and frameless tinted shades.

With her unique ensembles, she even recently inspired an online trend: “Is it a fit or is it just Bella Hadid?” (one of multiple spin-offs of “Is it a fit or is she just skinny?”). But even despite what may be perceived as merely random fashion choices, the supermodel's personal style philosophy centers on radiating beauty from within. “The best way to express inner beauty is through love and compassion,” Hadid told Bazaar.com.
