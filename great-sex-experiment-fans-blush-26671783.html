Great Sex Experiment fans blush over steamy threesome scene

A married couple had a red-hot threesome in front of the cameras on a Channel 4 documentary – and it got viewers' pulses racing.

Mike and Jess appeared on Friday's episode (April 8) of Open House: The Great Sex Experiment where couples dip into bringing other lovers into the bedroom.

The couple, who met nine years ago and have been married for five, work as full-time webcam models where they record their bedroom antics for the viewing pleasure of fans.

Jess said: "We pretty much have sex five days a week, every week."

Admitting they are very keen to try their first-ever threesome with another woman, they mingle at a drinks event and immediately feel a mutual attraction to 37-year-old Precious.
