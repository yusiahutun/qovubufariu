Janis Oliver Gill biography

Janis Oliver was born on 1st March 1954 in South Bay, California. She grew up in Manhattan alongside her younger sister Kristine Arnold. Oliver discovered her musical talent in elementary school, where she practised playing the guitar and also honed her skills in singing.

In their teenage years, Janis alongside her sister staged many live performances as back-up musicians. The duo later formed Sweethearts of the Rodeo, a band that would later flip America's music scene. The siblings rose to fame with each music they released. This meant every record company was queuing to sign them.

Shortly after becoming famous, Janis and Kristine launched a clothing line where they maximized their brand name. However, their busy schedules, including live performances and world tours, overwhelmed them, and they decided to close down the store.
